
# PICC Elders Room Launch Plan (ACT-ER)

**Project Code:** ACT-ER
**Parent Project:** ACT-PI (Palm Island Community Company)
**Launch Window:** February 7-12, 2026
**Status:** Planning Phase

## Overview

The Elders Room launch at Palm Island represents a significant milestone for PICC - a dedicated space honouring community elders through storytelling, portraits, and cultural preservation. The centerpiece will be the premiere of the Hull River Trip elders video, capturing elders sharing their stories and knowledge on country.

## Key Deliverables

### 1. Hull River Elders Video Premiere
- **What:** Documentary footage from the Hull River trip featuring elders sharing cultural knowledge and stories
- **Format:** Community screening at the Elders Room launch
- **Audience:** Elders, families, PICC board, community members
- **Action:** Confirm video edit status, prepare screening equipment

### 2. Elder Portraits for Display
- **What:** Printed portrait photographs of community elders for permanent display
- **Printing:** High-quality archival prints (canvas or framed)
- **Quantity:** TBC based on available portraits from Photo Studio (ACT-PS)
- **Action:** Review Photo Studio archives, select portraits, arrange printing
- **Source:** PICC On Country Photo Studio (ACT-PS) materials

### 3. Elders Room Naming Consultation
- **What:** Engage elders in naming their room - meaningful cultural name
- **Process:**
  - Gather name suggestions from elders
  - Discuss cultural significance of proposed names
  - Community vote or elder consensus
  - Create signage with English and traditional language
- **Action:** Schedule elder consultation sessions during visit

### 4. Elder Quotes Collection
- **What:** Gather meaningful quotes from elders for the launch and ongoing display
- **Uses:**
  - Wall displays in Elders Room
  - Annual Report content
  - Community communications
  - Storytelling preservation
- **Format:** Text + audio recordings (with permission)
- **Action:** Prepare quote collection framework, consent forms

### 5. Annual Report Connection
- **What:** Feature the Elders Room launch in PICC Annual Report
- **Content:** Photos, quotes, launch event coverage
- **Timeline:** Capture during Feb visit for 2025-26 report
- **Repository:** palm-island-repository (automated report system)

## Launch Event Ideas

### Cultural Continuity Focus
The launch should emphasize the importance of elders passing on knowledge to younger generations.

#### Intergenerational Elements
1. **Kids Art Activity** - Children create artwork inspired by elder stories
2. **Story Circle** - Elders share one story each with young people present
3. **Digital Archive Introduction** - Show how stories are being preserved
4. **Portrait Unveiling Ceremony** - Elders unveil their own portraits

#### Meaningful Touches
1. **Welcome to Country** - Traditional owner opening
2. **Smoking Ceremony** - Blessing the space (if culturally appropriate)
3. **Elder Blessing** - Senior elder formally opens the room
4. **Music/Song** - Traditional songs or contemporary Indigenous music
5. **Food** - Community catering, potentially involving elders' recipes

#### Ongoing Use Ideas
1. **Weekly Elder Gatherings** - Regular meeting space
2. **Story Recording Sessions** - Ongoing capture with Empathy Ledger
3. **Youth Mentoring** - Structured intergenerational programs
4. **Cultural Education** - Space for teaching language, customs

## Cultural Protocols

**Indigenous Data Sovereignty:** All stories, images, and recordings remain owned by the community and individuals. ACT acts as custodian, not owner.

**Consent:** Written consent required for all portraits, quotes, and recordings.

**Elder Respect:** Elders should have final say on what is shared publicly.

**Language:** Include traditional language names where appropriate.
