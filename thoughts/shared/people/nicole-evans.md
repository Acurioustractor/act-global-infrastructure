## Understanding — drafted
Nicole Evans is a seasoned medical educator and co-founder of ConnectedLe, an organization focused on mental health and wellbeing training. With more than two decades of experience in her field, she has transitioned to Bond University where she facilitates small group learning sessions. Nicole’s background at Griffith University's School of Medicine highlights her deep commitment to interactive and impactful educational experiences. In addition to her work in medical education, Nicole is also a keynote speaker and connection consultant, showcasing her multifaceted role in the community.

Nicole cares deeply about mental health and fostering meaningful connections with others. Her involvement in various roles such as a love and grief celebrant and life coach further underscores her dedication to supporting individuals on both personal and professional levels. She values interactive learning environments and aims to create impactful educational experiences that resonate with her students and participants. Nicole’s passion for nature and writing, evident from her work "Love Lessons," reflects her holistic approach to wellbeing and personal growth.
