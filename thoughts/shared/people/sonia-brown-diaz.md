## Understanding — drafted
Sonia Brown-Diaz is an advocate for mindfulness and yoga as a pathway to healing, particularly within incarcerated populations in Australia. She founded Yoga on the Inside, which offers trauma-informed yoga programs designed to support those who are incarcerated by providing them with tools for self-healing through mindfulness practices. In her role as the Australian Ambassador for the Prison Yoga Project, Sonia works tirelessly to promote and expand these initiatives across various correctional facilities.

Sonia cares deeply about addressing the impacts of trauma on individuals' lives and believes strongly in the transformative power of yoga and mindfulness. Her personal history with trauma has informed and driven her work, making it more empathetic and grounded in real-life experiences. By focusing on incarcerated individuals, she aims to provide them with resources for healing that can empower them both during their incarceration and after release, fostering a greater sense of wellbeing and resilience.
