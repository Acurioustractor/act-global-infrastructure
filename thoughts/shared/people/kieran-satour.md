Kieran Satour is a co-founder of GARUWA, a First Nations-led production company that prioritizes documentary storytelling, climate action, and social justice. His work focuses on elevating cultural humility and incorporating First Nations principles into the projects they undertake. Kieran aims to empower the next generation of storytellers and foster systemic change through his efforts at GARUWA.

By amplifying marginalized voices and addressing social justice issues, Kieran seeks to reclaim narrative control and promote equitable systems change.
