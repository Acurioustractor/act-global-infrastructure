## Understanding — drafted
Kieran Satour is a multifaceted individual deeply rooted in his cultural heritage as a member of the Gurindji/Malngin, Pertame Arrernte, and Worimi nations. He has made significant contributions to preserving and promoting Indigenous culture through his roles at Garuwa, an Aboriginal-owned creative agency he co-founded with his sister. Alongside Garuwa, Kieran also co-founded Indigitek, another platform focused on First Nations storytelling across diverse mediums such as film, which highlights the resilience and strength of Indigenous peoples in the face of colonial adversity.

Kieran cares deeply about narrative sovereignty and ensuring that Indigenous stories are told authentically by their own people. This passion is evident through his work at Garuwa and Indigitek, where he employs various creative mediums to celebrate and share First Nations culture. His efforts reflect a commitment to elevating the voices of Indigenous communities and fostering understanding and respect for their heritage among wider audiences.
