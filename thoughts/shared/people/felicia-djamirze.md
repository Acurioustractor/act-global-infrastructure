## Understanding — drafted
Felicia Djamirze is a trauma therapist and advocate who has dedicated herself to helping individuals, particularly former criminals, navigate the complexities of reintegration into society. She holds multiple roles as a contributing writer for About Time Media, CEO of Neuro Vitality, and founder of The Trauma Recovery Network. Her journey includes overcoming personal challenges, as she has been both a beauty queen and someone who has faced legal convictions herself. Through her experiences and professional work, Felicia aims to provide support and resources to those struggling with trauma recovery.

Felicia cares deeply about issues related to trauma recovery and criminal reform. She believes in the potential for individuals to change and reintegrate successfully into society despite their past actions. Her advocacy extends beyond just therapy; she is also an active voice in advocating for systemic changes that can better support those who are often marginalized or unfairly judged due to their past convictions. By sharing her own story and working through organizations like Neuro Vitality, Felicia strives to create a more empathetic and understanding society where rehabilitation and second chances are valued over judgment and stigma.
