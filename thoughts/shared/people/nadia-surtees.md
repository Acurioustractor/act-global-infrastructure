## Understanding — drafted
Nadia Surtees is a Product Design Lead at Canva, where she champions human-centered design principles to enhance user experience. Her background in psychology and interaction design allows her to create products that not only meet but anticipate users' needs. Nadia leverages methods from IDEO, a renowned global design firm, to ensure the designs are deeply empathetic and community-focused. She has cultivated a strong professional network and is recognized for her expertise in integrating user feedback into innovative solutions.

Nadia cares deeply about fostering meaningful connections between products and their users through empathy and community impact. Her work at Canva emphasizes the importance of involving real users throughout the design process to ensure that the outcomes are both impactful and relevant. She values a collaborative approach, drawing on insights from psychology and research to drive her designs. Nadia is particularly passionate about using design as a tool for positive change and enhancing user engagement by creating intuitive and accessible experiences.
