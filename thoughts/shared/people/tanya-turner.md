## Understanding — drafted
Tanya Turner is an Operations Manager at Oonchiumpa Consultancy & Services, based in Alice Springs, Northern Territory, Australia. She plays a pivotal role within the organization by translating community needs into actionable agreements and securing necessary funding for various initiatives. Tanya is particularly dedicated to youth justice advocacy, leveraging her background to ensure that young people have access to fair treatment and opportunities under the legal system.

Tanya cares deeply about justice and community empowerment, especially among youth. As a proud Aboriginal woman from Central Australia with a history of resilience, she uses her experiences and insights to navigate complex legal and policy systems. Her work aims to create meaningful change by ensuring that community voices are heard and incorporated into broader societal reforms. Tanya’s efforts highlight her commitment to not only advocate for but also actively support the growth and well-being of young people in her community.
