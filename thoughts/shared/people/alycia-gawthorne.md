## Understanding — drafted
Alycia Gawthorne is a dedicated advocate for refugee and asylum seeker rights in Australia. Her work with organizations such as GetUp and the Asylum Seeker Resource Centre involves strategic advocacy and communications to raise awareness about the challenges faced by refugees and asylum seekers. Alycia plays an integral role in developing and implementing publicity campaigns that aim to change public perceptions and promote more compassionate policies towards these communities.

She is passionate about ensuring that refugees and asylum seekers have access to the resources and support they need, and she works tirelessly to foster understanding and empathy within the broader community. Her efforts are focused on driving meaningful change through advocacy and public engagement, aiming to influence policy makers and the general public alike towards more humane approaches in dealing with these issues.
