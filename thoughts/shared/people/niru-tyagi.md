## Understanding — drafted
Niru Tyagi is the Principal Consultant and Founder of WHS Guard, an organization dedicated to workplace health and safety. As a systems thinker-in-residence, Niru focuses on creating environments where both physical and mental well-being are prioritized through robust risk management strategies and strict adherence to legislative compliance standards. His work involves advocating for a cultural shift in workplaces, moving beyond mere awareness of mental health issues to true accountability and action. Through his contributions to the WHS Guard newsletter and other platforms, Niru shares insights on contemporary challenges such as psychosocial hazards and new frontiers in workplace safety.

Niru cares deeply about fostering a culture where informed decision-making is at the forefront of preventing harm in workplaces. He emphasizes the importance of moving beyond just filling roles or gatekeeping practices to truly embodying the principles of risk management and legislative frameworks. His commitment to this cause is evident through his work and public commentary, highlighting the need for careful assessment and proactive measures that ensure everyone’s safety and well-being at work. Niru values environments where people genuinely care about safety and are committed to making a difference beyond just talking about it.
