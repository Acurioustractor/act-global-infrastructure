## Understanding — drafted
Alli Edwards is a Senior Programme Manager at the Centre for Public Impact, focusing on transformative learning and personal storytelling. With her background in both production and facilitation, Alli brings an innovative approach to impactful public policy and community engagement, particularly emphasizing playful and relational methods of learning. She has been actively involved in projects that aim to foster civic participation through empathetic understanding.

Alli cares deeply about using storytelling as a means to connect people and drive meaningful change within communities. Her work is centered around the belief that personal narratives can create deeper empathy and more engaged citizenry, ultimately leading to more effective public policies. She is dedicated to creating spaces where transformative learning can occur in ways that are both engaging and relational.

Our shared work with Alli has primarily revolved around exploring how civic participation can be enhanced through an initiative called the "Empathy Ledger" in Shepparton. Through our recent emails, we have been discussing strategies and methods for implementing this project, focusing on how to use empathy as a tool to increase community engagement and understanding.
