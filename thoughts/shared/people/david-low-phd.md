## Understanding — drafted
David Low, a PhD holder in environmental sciences, is the founder of the Australian Pesticide Research Network (APRN). His organization focuses on reducing pesticide pollution to protect both human health and the environment. David’s work involves promoting eco-literacy and improving scientific communication to ensure that complex issues related to pesticides are accessible to the public and policymakers alike.

David seems deeply committed to understanding and mitigating the adverse effects of pesticides, a passion that was kindled after his experience working at Monsanto and engaging with communities affected by pesticide use. His dedication extends beyond research; he advocates for policies that minimize pesticide usage without compromising agricultural productivity or environmental sustainability. Through APRN, David aims to create a more informed society capable of making healthier choices regarding chemical use in agriculture and other sectors.
