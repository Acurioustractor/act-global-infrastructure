Timothy Rosenlund-O’Brien is a prominent figure in Australia known for his deep expertise in systems finance, environmental, social, and governance (ESG) standards, and impact measurement. His work extends beyond advisory roles; he is also the founder of Good Measure, a tool designed to provide clearer insights into measuring social and environmental impacts.

Timothy appears to care deeply about enabling organizations and leaders to act with greater transparency and accountability in their efforts towards sustainability and social impact. He advocates for methods that remove barriers to entry and empower leaders with the tools necessary to measure and demonstrate the genuine impact of their actions on society and the environment.
