## Understanding — drafted
Penny Lamaro serves as the Manager at Women's Shelter Armidale Inc, where she leads efforts to support women who are survivors of domestic violence. Her work focuses on enhancing outcomes for these individuals by providing them with a safe environment and essential resources. Penny is deeply committed to advocating for those often overlooked in society and has been recognized for her contributions, notably being named the Northern Tablelands Woman of the Year.

Penny cares deeply about creating inclusive communities where women feel safe and supported. She aims to bridge gaps between victims of domestic violence and the justice system, ensuring that survivors have a voice in their own recovery journeys. By relocating the Women's Shelter office to a more accessible location in the mall area, Penny is also working to improve the accessibility of support services for those in need. Her dedication extends beyond her role at the shelter, as she has been involved with other organizations such as Ascent Group Australia Ltd, demonstrating a broader commitment to community welfare and leadership.
