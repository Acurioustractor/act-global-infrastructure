## Understanding — drafted
Canva is an online design tool designed to enable users to create visual content with ease. The platform caters to individuals as well as teams of various sizes, offering roles such as Canva Teams, Canva Business, and Canva Enterprise which are tailored to support collaboration within organizations while maintaining brand consistency across all created materials. Canva places a strong emphasis on making design accessible to everyone, regardless of their technical background or expertise in graphic design.

Canva cares deeply about fostering a supportive and inclusive environment for its users and employees alike. The company’s culture values the human element above all else, aiming to ensure that both its team members and customers feel supported and empowered. This commitment is evident not only through the platform's user-friendly interface but also through Canva's efforts to create an engaging and enjoyable workplace atmosphere in Sydney, where the company emphasizes collaboration and innovation among its diverse workforce.
