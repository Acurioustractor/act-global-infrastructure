## Understanding — drafted
Greg Graham is an influential figure in the advertising industry with a long-standing career spanning over three decades. He has held significant leadership roles at various companies, including Mindshare where he launched its Melbourne office as Managing Director and later worked under WPP/GroupM for more than 25 years. Currently, Greg serves on the Advisory Board of Advertising Industry Careers (AIC), an organization dedicated to fostering diverse and inclusive career opportunities within the advertising sector.

Greg Graham is particularly passionate about promoting diversity and inclusion in the media workforce and advertising industry at large. He founded The Experience Advocacy Taskforce (EAT), which advocates for a more equal and diverse environment that values all individuals, especially focusing on issues related to ageism. His work emphasizes the importance of creating an inclusive space where everyone's experiences are recognized and valued, reflecting his commitment to fostering a more equitable industry.
