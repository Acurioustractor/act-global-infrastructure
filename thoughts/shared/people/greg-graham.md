## Understanding — drafted
Greg Graham is a notable figure within the advertising and media industry, recognized for his significant contributions and leadership roles at WPP AUNZ and GroupM. He serves on the advisory board for Advertising Industry Careers (AIC), an organization dedicated to shaping careers in advertising by offering guidance and support to individuals entering or advancing their career paths within this competitive field.

Greg's work is marked by a strong advocacy for diversity and equal opportunities, as exemplified through his involvement with the Experience Advocacy Taskforce (EAT). This taskforce aims to create a more inclusive media workforce and promote fairness in advertising. Greg’s dedication reflects his deep-seated belief in fostering an industry that values inclusivity and equitable practices, ensuring that talent from all backgrounds has the opportunity to thrive.
