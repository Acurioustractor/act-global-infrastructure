## Understanding — drafted
Lorraine Dupree serves as the Executive Director of the Queensland Youth Housing Coalition (QYHC), an organization dedicated to addressing youth homelessness and providing safe housing for young people in Queensland. With over 26 years of experience, her expertise spans both media and social services sectors. Lorraine’s work is deeply rooted in supporting at-risk youth through policy advocacy, community engagement, and direct service provision aimed at ensuring that young Queenslanders have access to stable and secure living environments.

Lorraine cares passionately about creating systemic changes that can prevent homelessness among young people by addressing the root causes of instability and vulnerability. She is dedicated to fostering partnerships with various stakeholders including government agencies, non-profits, and community organizations to develop comprehensive solutions that support youth in need. Her commitment extends beyond direct service provision; she aims to raise awareness and advocate for policy changes that can lead to a more robust and accessible homelessness system for young Queenslanders.
