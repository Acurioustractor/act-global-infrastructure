## Understanding — drafted
Bounce is an organization dedicated to supporting chronically ill children and their families, providing both emotional and practical assistance. They operate a global storage network that aims to simplify the daily lives of those they support, making it easier for families to manage their belongings while navigating the challenges associated with chronic illness. Bounce emphasizes creating fun and impactful experiences for the children in their care, ensuring that despite their health struggles, life remains vibrant and full of meaningful moments.

Bounce cares deeply about building resilience within the families they serve and strives to create a sense of community where everyone feels supported and valued. They value the happiness and well-being of both those they support and their employees, focusing on fostering an environment where people can grow and thrive personally and professionally. Their commitment to positive change and teaching essential life skills demonstrates their dedication to making a lasting difference in the lives of chronically ill children and their families around the world.
