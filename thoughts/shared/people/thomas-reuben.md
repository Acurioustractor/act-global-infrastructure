Thomas Reuben serves as an ambassador for the Hydrospatial Movement Club & Community (HMCC), a relatively new organization established in 2020 with a focus on sustainable development through marine and coastal data. The HMCC is composed of twelve dedicated volunteers from around the world who are passionate about expanding knowledge related to hydrography and blue economy growth, emphasizing the importance of data-driven insights for the preservation and sustainable use of our oceans and coastal regions.

Thomas and his colleagues at the HMCC seem deeply committed to fostering a greater understanding of marine ecosystems through detailed data analysis. They care about leveraging expertise in geospatial information to advocate for sustainable practices that can benefit both the environment and economic growth, particularly within the context of the blue economy. Their efforts reflect a broad concern for environmental stewardship and the long-term viability of coastal and oceanic resources.
