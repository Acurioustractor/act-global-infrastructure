Kristen Lark is the CEO of Colliers Engineering & Design, a company known for its innovative and multidisciplinary approach to design. Her background includes significant roles such as District Ranger for Six Rivers National Forest and Senior Vice President at Fleishman Hillard, where she managed high-profile clients like Visa, Netflix, and Dell. Kristen is deeply committed to mission-driven initiatives and meaningful giving, often discussing these topics in her public appearances.

Kristen cares about fostering genuine connections that lead to impactful community involvement. She believes that true philanthropy stems from a deep sense of belief and connection rather than just financial contributions. Her work at Colliers Engineering & Design and her advocacy for meaningful causes reflect this philosophy, emphasizing the importance of aligning business practices with social responsibility.

In our recent interactions, we have been invited to participate in two events organized by Kristen. Through these engagements, it's clear that our shared work revolves around supporting community-driven initiatives aimed at empowering specific groups through meaningful events and gatherings.
