## Understanding — drafted
Angelique Wan serves as the CEO and co-founder of Consent Labs, a youth-led not-for-profit organization based in Australia. Her organization is dedicated to providing consent education that aims to empower young individuals in building healthy relationships and preventing sexual violence through informed knowledge and respectful interactions. The genesis of Consent Labs can be traced back to the founders' university experiences where they observed the normalization of sexual violence among young people, prompting them to take action.

Angelique appears deeply committed to fostering an environment where respect and consent are core values that guide interpersonal relationships. She believes in equipping young Australians with the knowledge and tools necessary to recognize and uphold these principles from a young age. This commitment extends beyond just imparting knowledge; it is about fundamentally changing societal attitudes towards consent, starting with education at school levels to ensure lasting cultural shifts. Her work underscores her dedication to empowering youth and creating a safer community through proactive educational efforts.
