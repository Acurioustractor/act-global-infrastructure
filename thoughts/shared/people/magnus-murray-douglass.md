## Understanding — drafted
Magnus Murray-Douglass is the co-founder of BOE Design, an organization that specializes in transforming waste materials into sustainable and functional products. Specifically, they repurpose plastic bottle caps collected from post-consumer sources into durable goods, contributing significantly to reducing environmental waste. Magnus focuses on sustainable design, using his expertise to turn discarded items into valuable resources.

Magnus cares deeply about the environment and the impact of human activities on it. His work at BOE Design is a reflection of this commitment to sustainability and waste reduction. Beyond just designing products, he emphasizes creating meaningful change through design. He also values community engagement, as evidenced by his involvement in community fundraising events and competitions that bring together designers and problem solvers to tackle real-world issues collaboratively.
