Talia Liddle is an Arrernte, Luritja, and Yankunytjatjara woman based in Alice Springs who works as a Video Production Coordinator for Common Ground First Nations. Her role involves amplifying Indigenous voices through various media projects, leveraging her experience across youth work, podcasting, documentary production, radio hosting, and presenting. Talia has made significant contributions to creative storytelling, particularly evident in her work on the environmental documentary "Larapinta," where she served as a presenter, co-director, producer, editor, and sound designer.

Talia seems deeply committed to using media platforms to educate and raise awareness about Indigenous culture, environment, and community issues. Her involvement in projects like "Larapinta" highlights her passion for environmental conservation and the importance of sharing traditional knowledge with broader audiences. Additionally, through her work at Common Ground First Nations, Talia aims to strengthen community connections and enhance tourism by showcasing Indigenous stories and perspectives, reflecting a strong dedication to cultural preservation and empowerment.
