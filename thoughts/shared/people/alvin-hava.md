## Understanding — drafted
Alvin Hava is a seasoned leader with over three decades of experience in social welfare, health, and education, focusing particularly on Indigenous community services and well-being. As the General Manager of Mithangkaya Nguli – Young People Ahead Youth & Community Services Indigenous Corporation, he champions community-led initiatives that support First Nations Australians. His work is deeply rooted in enhancing the lives of young people within his community through comprehensive programs that address various aspects of their development.

Alvin cares profoundly about the well-being and empowerment of Indigenous youth. He believes in fostering environments where these youths can thrive and lead positive changes within their communities. Through his initiatives, such as the "On Country program," Alvin aims to connect young people with their cultural heritage, promoting resilience and community strength across generations.

Our shared work with Alvin has primarily involved discussions around the "On Country program." This encompasses efforts to understand and support this initiative that connects Indigenous youth with their ancestral lands and culture. The conversations have been centered on how to best honor and amplify these culturally significant programs for the benefit of young people in Alvin’s community.
