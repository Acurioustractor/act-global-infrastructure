## Understanding — drafted
Cath Manuel is a dedicated horticulturist and therapeutic horticulture specialist who focuses on utilizing gardening to promote health, wellness, and quality of life. Her work encompasses various aspects, from guiding individuals in creating thriving gardens that are accessible to all, including those with dementia, to sharing simple yet effective gardening ideas through her YouTube channel "Soil to Supper." Cath is committed to making gardening a source of enjoyment and personal development, particularly emphasizing its therapeutic benefits.

Cath cares deeply about the intersection between nature and human well-being. She not only advocates for accessible gardening practices but also actively supports companion animals by donating proceeds from her monthly market stall to their care. This reflects her broader commitment to fostering connections—both between people and plants as well as among different forms of life—that can lead to improved health outcomes and enhanced community bonds. Her efforts are grounded in a genuine desire to spread positivity through the act of gardening, making it accessible and beneficial for everyone involved.
