In this role, she plays a pivotal part in enhancing the graduate student experience through various initiatives that aim to foster engagement, advocacy, and support services. Her background encompasses not only academic engagement but also includes roles as a fraud prevention speaker and resilience expert, highlighting her dedication to supporting individuals in facing and overcoming various challenges. Through her diverse experiences and professional endeavors, Kerry shows a clear passion for helping others build strength and overcome obstacles, both in the academic realm and beyond.
