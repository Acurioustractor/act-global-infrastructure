## Understanding — drafted
Aleisha Lensink (Hudson) serves as the Head of Operational Efficiency at Catholic Early EdCare, where she champions a child-first approach in early education settings. Her role emphasizes ensuring that children's needs are prioritized over administrative tasks, advocating for environments that foster both their academic and social development. Aleisha has been featured on podcasts discussing how to implement such approaches effectively within early childhood care programs, highlighting the importance of reducing unnecessary paperwork and focusing on direct support for children.

Aleisha cares deeply about strengthening the connection between educational institutions and communities, recognizing that a collaborative effort is essential for nurturing young minds. She values academic excellence but also underscores the significance of community engagement in supporting families and ensuring that students have pathways to future success. Her efforts extend beyond her immediate role at Catholic Early EdCare, as evidenced by her involvement with Hudson Catholic, where she has supported initiatives aimed at mentoring and spiritually enriching the student body through campus ministry activities.
