## Understanding — drafted
Sheryl Batchelor is the founder and Chief Executive Officer of Yiliyapinya Indigenous Corporation, a not-for-profit organization dedicated to enhancing brain health among Indigenous Australians. With over 30 years of experience in education and research, Sheryl has worked diligently to integrate Aboriginal knowledge with contemporary neuroplasticity programs aimed at improving memory, attention, and social skills. As a proud Kunja woman, she brings a unique perspective that enriches her work and supports the broader goals of improving quality of life for Indigenous communities.

Sheryl cares deeply about leveraging scientific advancements in brain health to address historical and ongoing challenges faced by Indigenous Australians. Her commitment extends beyond professional boundaries; she is passionate about honoring Aboriginal heritage while using modern research to create meaningful change. This dual focus on traditional knowledge and innovative science reflects her dedication to empowering individuals within her community through improved cognitive functions, ultimately aiming for a more holistic enhancement of their well-being and societal integration.
