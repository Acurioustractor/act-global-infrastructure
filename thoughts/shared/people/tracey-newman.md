## Understanding — drafted
Tracey Newman is the Managing Director & Head of Impact at Impacto Consulting, an organization dedicated to high-impact consulting projects. With a background that includes roles with ImpaxSIA Consulting and the Animal Justice Party South Australia, she brings a diverse range of experiences to her current position. Tracey’s career has also seen her work in political campaigning through Newman Partners, a firm she founded focused on impactful political campaigns in Washington State. Since Paper Giant acquired Impacto Consulting, Tracey continues to lead with her expertise and commitment to high-impact solutions.

Tracey cares deeply about building strong relationships and co-creating effective solutions that have a significant impact on the systems they serve. Her approach involves focusing closely on employers who can make substantial contributions to systemic change. This focus highlights her dedication not only to achieving impactful outcomes but also to fostering collaboration and partnership in the process of creating those solutions.
