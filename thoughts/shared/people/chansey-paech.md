## Understanding — drafted
Chansey Paech is a prominent figure in the Northern Territory’s political landscape, serving as a Member of the Legislative Assembly for Gwoja since 2016. He was elected Speaker of the Legislative Assembly in 2020 and previously represented Namatjira. As an Eastern Arrernte and Gurindji man born and raised in Alice Springs, Chansey brings a unique perspective to his role, focusing on justice reform and advocating for Central Australians. His tenure has been marked by efforts to ensure that remote communities have access to the same opportunities as urban areas.

Chansey Paech is deeply committed to community advocacy and social equity, particularly for Aboriginal people in Central Australia. He cares about improving legal and constitutional affairs, health services, mental health support, disability care, and senior welfare within his constituency. His work reflects a dedication to addressing systemic issues and fostering inclusive policies that benefit all residents of the Northern Territory. Through his roles in both legislative committees and shadow ministries, Chansey aims to create tangible changes that resonate with the community’s needs and aspirations.
