Tom Gibson is the founder and creative director of Woofys Group, a company based in Eveleigh, New South Wales, Australia. He launched his first venture, Woofys Fun Food, at Bondi Beach in January 2013 as an innovative gourmet sausage sizzle hybrid, which quickly evolved into the broader experiential marketing firm that Woofys Group is today. His background includes work as the Art Director at DDB Germany and a stint with Two Hands Agency before founding his own company. Woofys Group specializes in connecting brands with people through engaging and memorable experiences, often centered around premium, locally sourced food and drink offerings.
