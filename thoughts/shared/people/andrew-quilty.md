## Understanding — drafted
Andrew Quilty is an Australian photojournalist who has dedicated himself to documenting the realities of life and conflict in Afghanistan. Based in Kabul since 2013, he captures the complex narratives of a nation embroiled in war, aiming to bridge the gap between distant audiences and the often overlooked truths of the region. His work spans various media, including his book "This is Afghanistan: 2014 - 2021" and a podcast, which delve into the human aspects of the conflict and its aftermath.

Andrew clearly cares deeply about highlighting the human cost of war and fostering empathy among those who may not have direct experience with such conflicts. Through his photography and multimedia projects, he strives to present an unvarnished view of Afghanistan's current events, aiming to educate and connect people across cultures and continents. His work underscores a commitment to truth and a desire for greater understanding and peace.
