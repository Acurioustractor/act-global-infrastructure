## Understanding — drafted
Alison Harrington is the CEO and founder of Resparke, a company dedicated to enhancing the lives of individuals living with dementia through innovative therapeutic methods. Initially founded under the name Moove and Groove, Alison has evolved her venture into Resparke, which integrates music and movement into care routines for people suffering from dementia. Her work leverages technology to create immersive experiences that can significantly improve the quality of life for those affected by dementia.

Alison’s approach is deeply personal, inspired by her own experiences and driven by a genuine passion for improving care within aged care facilities. She cares profoundly about harnessing the therapeutic power of music and movement to not only alleviate symptoms but also provide joy and connection to individuals with dementia. Her efforts aim to support patients, their loved ones, and caretakers alike, creating a more compassionate and effective system of care that recognizes the dignity and humanity of each individual.
