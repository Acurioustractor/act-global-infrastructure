## Understanding — drafted
Dr. Joanne Stone is a distinguished leader in maternal-fetal medicine and obstetrics, currently serving as the System Chair of Obstetrics, Gynecology, and Reproductive Science at Mount Sinai's Icahn School of Medicine. Her work involves advancing knowledge through research on invasive fetal procedures and multifetal pregnancies. Dr. Stone’s expertise extends beyond clinical practice; she is also an associate editor for the American Journal of Obstetrics & Gynecology, reflecting her significant contributions to academic publishing in women's health.

Dr. Stone cares deeply about improving maternal and fetal outcomes through innovative medical practices and educational initiatives. She plays a pivotal role in organizing and promoting various training programs aimed at enhancing ultrasound techniques and knowledge among clinicians. Through her leadership and involvement with organizations like the GOHO Memorial Foundation, Dr. Stone supports fellowships and educational courses that empower healthcare professionals to deliver superior care for women and their babies. Her dedication is underscored by her commitment to both clinical excellence and continuous education in obstetrics and gynecology.
