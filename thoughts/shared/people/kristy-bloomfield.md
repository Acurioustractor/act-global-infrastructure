## Understanding — drafted
Kristy Bloomfield leads Oonchiumpa Consultancy & Services, an organization deeply rooted in Central Australia's Indigenous community. Her work centers around empowering Indigenous communities through the preservation and application of traditional knowledge and healing practices. As a Traditional Owner on Arrernte Country, Kristy stewards Elder councils and ensures the proper protocols for knowledge are respected and upheld. She is recognized as a visionary leader who advocates fervently for Indigenous empowerment and community development.

Kristy cares deeply about supporting young people in navigating their cultural transitions between traditional and contemporary worlds. Her work aims to challenge systemic barriers that impede this navigation, providing them with a strong foundation of cultural identity and resilience. By bridging the gap between old and new ways of life, she seeks to foster healing within her community and promote a harmonious coexistence. Through her efforts at Oonchiumpa Consultancy & Services, Kristy honors and amplifies Indigenous voices while advocating for their rights and well-being.
