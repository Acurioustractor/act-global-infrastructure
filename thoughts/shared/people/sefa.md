## Understanding — drafted
SEFA is an organization that fosters community involvement and leadership among state employees, particularly through donation campaigns. They work closely with the United Way to facilitate easy contributions for PEF members by enabling payroll deductions. SEFA emphasizes minimal administrative costs and efficient volunteer management, focusing on the spirit of giving back to the community. Their efforts have become integral to places like Buffalo State's culture, inspiring a broader sense of community among employees.

SEFA cares deeply about maintaining a low-cost structure while maximizing the impact of employee donations. This focus allows more resources to reach those in need and underscores their commitment to genuine community engagement and leadership development within state employment circles. They value volunteerism and see it as key to building strong, supportive communities both internally and externally.

Our shared work with SEFA has been marked by periodic updates about the organization's activities and progress throughout different seasons. In our communications, we exchanged information in spring and again in summer, covering topics like campaign updates and community impact highlights. These exchanges have allowed us to stay informed about SEFA’s ongoing efforts and their commitment to fostering a culture of giving among state employees.
