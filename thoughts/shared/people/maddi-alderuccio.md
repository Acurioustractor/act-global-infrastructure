Madeline Alderuccio, known as Maddi, is a Purpose and Impact Coordinator at The Funding Network Australia (TFN). Her role involves supporting social causes and community-led initiatives that aim to make meaningful impacts in local communities. Graduating from RMIT University in 2022, she has brought her passion for advancing social justice and an intersectional approach to her work with TFN. Maddi is particularly interested in fostering initiatives that address a range of issues while ensuring they are rooted in community needs.

Maddi cares deeply about the communities she serves and focuses on creating sustainable impacts through collaboration and support. She values the importance of local, community-led projects and aims to amplify their voices by providing them with resources and platforms to thrive. Her work at TFN revolves around connecting donors with impactful projects, ensuring that funding reaches initiatives focused on social justice and environmental sustainability.

Our shared work with Maddi has primarily involved tracking and updating the impact of grants provided through TFN’s programs, particularly focusing on a project titled "Healthy People Healthy Planet." We have collaborated on distributing funds to various community-led initiatives, monitoring their progress over six-month periods, and ensuring that all grant distributions are managed efficiently. Additionally, we’ve worked together on donor connections post-event activities, leveraging the enthusiasm generated at TFN events to continue fostering engagement and support for social causes.
