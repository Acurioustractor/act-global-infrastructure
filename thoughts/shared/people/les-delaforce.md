## Understanding — drafted
Les Delaforce is a prominent First Nations leader in innovation and environmental, social, and governance (ESG) practices, currently serving at Rio Tinto in their Research and Development department. His career revolves around fostering Indigenous entrepreneurship and nurturing startup ecosystems, with a particular emphasis on integrating technology and sustainable development principles into these initiatives. Les’s work at Rio Tinto complements his broader efforts to enhance the entrepreneurial environment for First Nations communities, particularly within Australia.

Les cares deeply about empowering Indigenous startups and leveraging technology to support sustainable growth in these enterprises. His commitment extends beyond just creating opportunities; he focuses on building ecosystems that are resilient and capable of thriving independently. This dedication is reflected in his extensive network and leadership roles, which include positions at both Rio Tinto and the Minderoo Foundation, where he aims to amplify Indigenous voices and initiatives within the startup community. Les’s work underscores a strong belief in the potential of First Nations entrepreneurship as a catalyst for broader economic and social development.
