## Understanding — drafted
Wilya Janta Accounts represents an Aboriginal not-for-profit organization dedicated to addressing housing challenges within remote Aboriginal communities. Their mission centers on developing solutions that are both community-led and culturally respectful, ensuring that the designs of their housing projects reflect local traditions and environmental conditions. By prioritizing cultural safety and climate-appropriate design principles, Wilya Janta aims to create living spaces that go beyond mere shelter; they seek to preserve and honor Aboriginal culture while providing practical and sustainable housing solutions.

The organization cares deeply about preserving Aboriginal culture through the physical environment of homes and communities. They are committed to ensuring that the built environment respects and incorporates traditional knowledge and practices, rather than imposing external models that might not be suitable for these communities. This approach reflects their dedication to community agency and cultural integrity, aiming to support a holistic well-being that encompasses both physical shelter and spiritual connectedness.
