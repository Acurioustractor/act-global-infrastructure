## Understanding — drafted
Rebecca Willis is a seasoned professional with over two decades of experience in human resources and recruitment. She founded TalentLink in 2020 with the mission to transform traditional recruitment methods into more engaging and supportive processes for both candidates and employers. Prior to launching TalentLink, Rebecca held roles as an HR manager and freelance writer, contributing her expertise through various content pieces and speaking engagements. Additionally, she has been involved in Tractus Learning as a modern learning strategist and chief learning officer.

Rebecca cares deeply about employee development and learning engagement within the workplace. Her work often revolves around strategies that empower employees to reach their full potential through coaching, modern learning approaches, and continuous development opportunities. She believes in creating environments where individuals can thrive, grow professionally, and contribute meaningfully to their organizations. Rebecca’s passion for these areas is evident through her extensive speaking engagements and content creation aimed at advancing the field of human resources and recruitment with innovative methods.
