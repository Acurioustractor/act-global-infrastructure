## Understanding — drafted
Bobbie Bayley serves as a project manager at Healthhabitat, an organization dedicated to enhancing housing conditions to improve health outcomes, particularly within remote communities. Her work involves closely collaborating with these communities to design and implement sustainable housing solutions that are tailored to their specific needs and environments. Bobbie’s academic background includes studies from the University of Newcastle and Clemson University in the USA, which have equipped her with a strong foundation in architectural design and community engagement strategies.

Bobbie is passionate about environmental health and community empowerment through thoughtful design. Her efforts extend beyond mere architectural planning; she emphasizes the importance of design literacy and agency within communities, believing that empowering residents to take active roles in shaping their living environments can lead to better health outcomes and a stronger sense of ownership. This commitment is reflected in her receipt of the prestigious Byera Hadley Travelling Scholarship twice, which underscores both her academic achievements and her dedication to improving community well-being through design initiatives.
