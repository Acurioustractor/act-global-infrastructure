## Understanding — drafted
Bobbie Bayley is a dedicated Project Manager for Healthabitat, an organization focused on improving living conditions through sustainable architecture. With academic credentials from the University of Newcastle and Clemson University, Bobbie specializes in enhancing thermal performance and sustainable architectural design. Her work primarily involves collaborating with remote communities to manage repairs and ensure that living spaces are comfortable and energy-efficient.

Bobbie cares deeply about making a tangible difference in the lives of people who reside in remote areas. She is committed to using her expertise in architecture to improve the thermal comfort and sustainability of buildings, which she believes can significantly enhance the quality of life for residents. Through her work with Healthabitat and Dogspike Design, Bobbie strives to empower communities by addressing housing issues that impact health and well-being. Her collaborative approach highlights a genuine interest in understanding community needs and implementing sustainable solutions that resonate with local contexts.
