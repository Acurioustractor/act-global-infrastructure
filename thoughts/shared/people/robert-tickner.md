## Understanding — drafted
Robert Tickner is the Chairperson of the Board for the Justice Reform Initiative, an organization dedicated to reducing Australia’s reliance on incarceration and advocating for more effective and humane justice system reforms. Since its establishment in September 2020, under Tickner's leadership, the initiative has focused on highlighting the detrimental impacts of over-incarceration not only on incarcerated individuals but also on their families and communities at large.

Tickner appears to care deeply about social reform and improving the justice system through a more compassionate lens. His work is driven by a belief that incarceration should be used as a last resort, emphasizing the importance of alternative methods for addressing crime and rehabilitation. Through his role with the Justice Reform Initiative, Tickner aims to foster a dialogue around these issues, advocating for systemic changes that can lead to a more equitable and just society.
