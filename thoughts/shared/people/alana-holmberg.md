## Understanding — drafted
Alana Holmberg is a multifaceted artist known for her evocative photography and storytelling that delves into issues of family, feminism, and body image. Her haunting imagery often serves as a medium to push social awareness on these topics, making her work both poignant and impactful. Beyond her photographic practice, Alana also works as a writer and has ventured into the realm of 360 VR short films, showcasing her versatility in different mediums.

Alana cares deeply about using her art to provoke thought and spark conversations around societal issues, particularly those affecting women and families. She values the importance of a supportive environment for artists and often reflects on how her past work can be reinterpreted through new perspectives. Her commitment to exploring diverse storytelling methods, from traditional photography to immersive VR experiences, underscores her dedication to creative innovation while maintaining a focus on social impact.
