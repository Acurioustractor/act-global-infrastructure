## Understanding — drafted
Dominic Brook serves as the Managing Director of Musicians Making a Difference (MMAD), a charity he co-founded with the mission to empower at-risk youth through music and mentoring. Based in Australia, MMAD leverages the transformative power of music to positively impact young lives that are often vulnerable or facing significant challenges. Through his work, Dominic aims to provide these youths with the support they need to navigate their difficulties and foster a sense of empowerment and self-worth.

Dominic appears deeply committed to using his platform not just to inspire but also to create meaningful change in the lives of the youth he serves. He cares about providing them with opportunities for personal growth, resilience, and confidence building through musical engagement. His work underscores a belief that music can serve as both a therapeutic tool and a means of self-expression, offering young people a pathway to navigate life's challenges more effectively and build stronger futures for themselves.
