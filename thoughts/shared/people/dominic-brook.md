## Understanding — drafted
Dominic Brook serves as the Managing Director of Musicians Making a Difference (MMAD), an organization he co-founded with the mission of leveraging music to empower young people, particularly those at risk. Through MMAD's programs, Dominic has reached tens of thousands of young Australians, providing them with opportunities to grow and find their voice through musical expression. His work is deeply rooted in his belief that every individual can contribute positively to society, regardless of their background.

Dominic appears to care profoundly about the well-being and potential of youth, especially those facing challenges such as poverty or social isolation. He values the power of music not only as a form of artistic expression but also as a tool for emotional healing and personal development. His efforts extend beyond just providing musical instruction; he aims to create an environment where young people can build confidence, develop critical life skills, and feel supported in their journey towards a better future.
