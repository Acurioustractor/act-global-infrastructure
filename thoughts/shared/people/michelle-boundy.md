## Understanding — drafted
Michelle Boundy is the founder of She Inspires Me Australia, an organization that aims to advance women's leadership and promote workplace safety across Australia. Established on International Women’s Day in 2023, She Inspires Me has quickly become a respected platform both within Australia and globally for recognizing inspiring women leaders. Michelle herself has over three decades of experience in various capacities, including government roles, and is acknowledged as an expert in workplace wellbeing. Her work continues to influence positive change in leadership and workplace culture.

Michelle cares deeply about creating environments where women can thrive and feel safe at work. Through her role as a National Work Health & Safety Ambassador for 2026 and her extensive background as a recognized subject matter expert, she has dedicated herself to advocating for better workplace conditions and nurturing the growth of women in leadership roles. Her efforts reflect a commitment not only to improving individual wellbeing but also to transforming organizational cultures towards greater inclusivity and safety.
