## Understanding — drafted
Natalie Chiappazzo is a seasoned social impact consultant with over 20 years of experience, focusing particularly on youth and community initiatives. She currently serves in a significant role at BYSA (Boys & Young Sisters Association) and holds a position as a board member for the Western Sydney Community Forum. Her work revolves around empowering young people to take an active role in shaping their futures and the services that support them.

Natalie cares deeply about youth agency, advocating for systems where young individuals can lead and make decisions that affect their lives. She believes in fostering environments where young people are not just recipients of programs but are actively involved in designing and implementing solutions that benefit their communities. Her dedication to this cause is evident through her active participation on the executive committee of Youth Work NSW, where she contributes to discussions and initiatives aimed at enhancing youth services and opportunities across New South Wales.
