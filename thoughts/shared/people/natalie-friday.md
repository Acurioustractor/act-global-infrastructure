## Understanding — drafted
Natalie Friday serves as the Ranger Coordinator for the MinggaMingga Rangers, an organization based on Palm Island with a focus on both land and sea conservation efforts. Under her leadership, the rangers actively engage in marine debris monitoring, which involves tracking and managing waste found within the waters surrounding Palm Island. This work is integral to their broader mission of sustainable environmental management that aims to preserve natural resources for future generations while respecting traditional practices.

Natalie appears to care deeply about blending modern scientific methods with Indigenous knowledge to create a balanced approach to conservation. She emphasizes unity and collaboration, not only in her team's environmental efforts but also in fostering opportunities for youth involvement in these critical tasks. Her work is grounded in a profound respect for the land and sea country that she and her team are dedicated to protecting, reflecting a commitment to sustainable practices that honor both cultural heritage and ecological stewardship.
