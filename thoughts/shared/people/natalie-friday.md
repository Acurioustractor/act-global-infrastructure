## Understanding — drafted
Natalie Friday serves as the Ranger Coordinator for the MinggaMingga Rangers on Palm Island, a position that allows her to blend traditional knowledge with modern environmental management practices. The rangers under her coordination are dedicated to protecting and preserving the marine environment surrounding Palm Island and its neighboring islands within the Great Barrier Reef World Heritage Area. Their work involves not only conservation efforts but also educational initiatives aimed at engaging and empowering local youth.

Natalie and the MinggaMingga Rangers clearly care deeply about maintaining a connection between their community's cultural heritage and environmental stewardship. This passion is reflected in their efforts to create sustainable pathways for young people, ensuring that they can carry forward both traditional ecological knowledge and contemporary conservation practices. By fostering this integration, Natalie and her team aim to nurture a new generation of custodians who are well-equipped to manage the delicate ecosystems of Palm Island and beyond.
