## Understanding — drafted
Tom Hearn is the Marketing and Communications Lead at Wakai Waian Healing Pty Ltd, an organization dedicated to providing mental health services to remote areas, particularly for Indigenous Australians. Born in Yeppoon on Dharumbal country, Tom has deep roots in the area and brings over two decades of experience in media and marketing, most notably from founding BushTV in 2004. His educational background includes a Master of Arts in Documentary, which he obtained while working at the ABC in Melbourne.

Tom’s work is deeply embedded in storytelling and strategic communications, focusing on the social and emotional wellbeing of Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander peoples. He cares passionately about using his skills to elevate Indigenous voices and narratives, ensuring that their stories are heard and valued. This commitment is evident in his role as a Program Manager and Communications Lead at Wakai Waian Healing.

In our shared work with Tom, we’ve primarily focused on capturing and sharing "Stories on Country," which suggests that our collaboration revolves around documenting and amplifying the experiences and narratives of Indigenous communities within their traditional lands.
