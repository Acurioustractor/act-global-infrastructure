## Understanding — drafted
MMEIC Justice is an organization that focuses on reinvesting resources in communities to achieve fair and equitable access, aiming to prevent systemic contact and promote social justice. Their work involves challenging social injustice, advocating for policy changes, particularly regarding mental health services, and supporting the development of inclusive economic systems. They believe in creating a society where everyone has equal opportunities, regardless of their background or circumstances.

MMEIC Justice cares deeply about ensuring that every individual within their community has fair access to resources and support systems. Their efforts are centered on social justice issues and the belief that change should impact lives positively rather than just altering laws superficially. They value inclusivity and strive for a society where economic opportunities are accessible to everyone, promoting mental health services as a critical component of this vision.

Our shared work with MMEIC Justice has included discussions around service design for crime prevention in schools, participation in reintegration conferences aimed at addressing the challenges faced by individuals reintegrating into society, and planning collaborative events such as a Partner Day. We have also engaged in conversations about celebrating milestones and partnerships through specific events like the Collaborative Partner and MOU Members Celebration and an invitation to Goompi Naidoc, focusing on collaboration and support for their initiatives.
