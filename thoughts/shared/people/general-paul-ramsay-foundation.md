## Understanding — drafted
The Paul Ramsay Foundation is a significant charitable organization in Australia, dedicated to ending disadvantage through innovative research and community engagement. The foundation aims to create conditions that enable people and communities to thrive by investing in and influencing various sectors and policies. It was established by philanthropist Paul Joseph Ramsay, who also founded Ramsay Health Care.

The Paul Ramsay Foundation cares deeply about fostering an environment where everyone has the opportunity to succeed, particularly focusing on reducing disadvantage through strategic investments and community partnerships. They value inclusivity and flexibility in their work approach, recognizing that their staff have diverse commitments outside of work.

Our shared work with the Paul Ramsay Foundation involves staying updated on various initiatives they are undertaking. This includes supporting national grant rounds for programs like Dads on Track, reviewing annual progress and achievements, promoting fellowship applications to engage new talent, and encouraging innovation by transforming ideas into meaningful change.
