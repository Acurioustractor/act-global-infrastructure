## Understanding — drafted
First Nations Futures is an organization that focuses on improving the economic and health outcomes for First Nations communities in Australia through a lens of justice and cultural safety. Their work involves redistributing wealth to community organizations, strengthening leadership within these communities, and ensuring healthcare practices are culturally sensitive to Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander needs. By working directly with communities and listening to their voices, they aim to shift power dynamics and create sustainable improvements that reflect the unique histories and cultures of First Nations people.

First Nations Futures clearly cares about empowering community-led initiatives and fostering a sense of autonomy among its members. Their focus on economic justice through wealth redistribution indicates a deep concern for addressing systemic inequalities faced by Indigenous communities. Additionally, their emphasis on cultural safety within healthcare shows they prioritize holistic well-being that includes both physical health and cultural integrity.

The shared work with First Nations Futures has primarily revolved around supporting their initiatives aimed at shifting power dynamics and building alliances. In 2025, there was a focus on reflecting upon the year's activities and setting intentions for redistribution of wealth starting in January 26, as highlighted by the "WEALTHBACK" initiative. The most recent communication emphasizes standing with the organization before June 30, suggesting ongoing support and partnership to advance their goals of economic justice and cultural empowerment.
