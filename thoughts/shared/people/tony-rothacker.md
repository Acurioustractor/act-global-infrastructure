## Understanding — drafted
Tony Rothacker is co-founder and leader at Youngster.co, an organization that he established to bridge the technological divide between younger and older generations. With a background as a civil engineer and experience founding an internet service provider and leading the Coffs Coast Innovation Hub, Tony has brought his expertise in technology and community building to create a platform where youth can gain job opportunities while providing tech mentoring services to elders. His co-founder is his son Finn, who serves as the Chief Technology Officer at Youngster.co.

Tony seems deeply committed to fostering intergenerational collaboration and empowering both young people and older adults through technology. He cares about creating meaningful connections that enhance the quality of life for all participants, ensuring that youth find purpose and employment while elders gain essential tech skills. This work is not just about teaching or learning but also about building stronger, more resilient communities where mutual support and understanding flourish across age groups.
