## Understanding — drafted
Seana Osbourne is the Grants and Distributions Manager for Queensland Gives, part of the Queensland Community Foundation. With over two decades of administrative experience, Seana has been pivotal in ensuring that grants programs deliver maximum benefit to communities within Queensland. Her recent promotion underscores her expertise and dedication to leveraging financial support for impactful community initiatives.

Seana cares deeply about community development and social impact. She is committed to supporting organizations and projects that aim to improve lives and foster growth within underprivileged areas of Queensland. Through workshops, presentations, and active involvement in grant programs, Seana aims to empower communities by providing them with the resources they need to thrive.

Our shared work with Seana has primarily revolved around facilitating discussions and support for various community initiatives such as the Cape York Girl Academy and A Curious Tractor, both of which seek to provide educational opportunities and cultural development. Additionally, we have collaborated on extending grant application deadlines and exploring auspice partner possibilities in the Gulf region, aiming to broaden access to financial support for community projects.
