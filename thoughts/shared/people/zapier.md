## Understanding — drafted
Zapier is a company dedicated to making automation accessible and efficient for businesses of all sizes. They offer tools that allow users to automate workflows across thousands of different apps, simplifying complex processes and enhancing productivity. A key part of their mission is not just to provide these tools but also to ensure they are easy for everyone to use. Zapier places significant emphasis on employee well-being and fosters a remote-first culture where open dialogue about representation and purpose is encouraged.

Zapier cares deeply about empowering businesses through automation while maintaining a strong focus on the welfare of its employees. They value transparency, equity, and a sense of purpose in their work environment, ensuring that each team member feels supported and motivated to do their best work. This commitment extends beyond just internal practices; Zapier also seeks to improve and refine industry standards for support and cultural practices.

In our interactions with Zapier over the past couple of weeks, we have been supporting them through their trial period by highlighting key features such as AI-powered workflows and popular automations used by teams. We’ve also guided them in building their first automation using Copilot, emphasizing how to effectively put AI to work for increasing efficiency.
