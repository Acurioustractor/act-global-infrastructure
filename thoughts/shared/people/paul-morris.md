## Understanding — drafted
Paul Morris is a Dunghutti/Wonnarua man who serves as a Board Member for the Aboriginal Legal Service (NSW/ACT) Limited, advocating for self-determination, social justice, and truth-telling. He plays a critical role in representing community voices within legal proceedings and has extensive experience in helping individuals navigate the complex legal system. Beyond his work at the Aboriginal Legal Service, Morris also serves as the Chief Executive Officer of Durri ACMS, where he focuses on local programs and lifelong care to strengthen his community.

Paul Morris cares deeply about empowering Indigenous communities through self-management and positive change. His efforts are directed towards ensuring that community voices are heard in legal matters and that there is a focus on social justice and truth-telling. Additionally, his interest in regenerative agriculture underscores his commitment to holistic community development, aiming to restore land, provide nutritious food, generate income, sequester carbon, and promote environmental sustainability. These initiatives reflect Morris’s dedication to the well-being of Indigenous communities not just legally but also environmentally and socially.
