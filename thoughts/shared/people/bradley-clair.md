## Understanding — drafted
Bradley Clair is a social impact entrepreneur who co-founded PowerWells, a company focused on addressing energy poverty and electronic waste. As CEO of PowerWells, he leads the organization in recycling lithium-ion batteries from discarded electronics to provide affordable solar energy solutions. This work not only helps to mitigate the environmental impacts of e-waste but also offers cheaper and sustainable alternatives to traditional energy sources for communities facing energy poverty.

Bradley clearly cares deeply about social and environmental issues, particularly where they intersect with technology and innovation. His work demonstrates a commitment to finding practical, sustainable solutions that can have a direct positive impact on people's lives. Bradley’s dedication is evident in his recognition as the QCF Innovation Philanthropist of the year in 2022, which reflects not only his entrepreneurial efforts but also his philanthropic contributions aimed at enhancing the well-being and opportunities for individuals around the world.
