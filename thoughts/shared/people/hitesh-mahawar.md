## Understanding — drafted
Hitesh Mahawar is the Climate Change Programme Manager at Cherwell District Council where he plays a crucial role in steering the council towards achieving its carbon net zero targets. His work involves overseeing and leading climate action initiatives that are designed to reduce the council's environmental footprint significantly by 2030. Hitesh’s responsibilities encompass developing corporate policies, managing programs aimed at mitigating climate change impacts, and fostering a culture of sustainability within Cherwell District Council.

Hitesh cares deeply about sustainability and the broader implications of climate change on communities. His work reflects his commitment to making tangible changes that can help local governments achieve their ambitious carbon net zero goals. He is dedicated to educating and informing stakeholders about the importance of environmental stewardship, aiming to mobilize collective action towards a more sustainable future. Through his role at Cherwell District Council, Hitesh seeks to create impactful strategies that not only address immediate climate challenges but also lay the groundwork for long-term resilience in the face of global environmental changes.
