## Understanding — drafted
Peter Spinda is the Partner & CEO at Benchmark Business Advisory in Brisbane, Queensland, Australia. With over two decades of professional experience, Peter focuses on advising business owners to increase their profits and maintain long-term relevance in the market. His work encompasses a range of strategies including reducing dependency within organizations, driving digital transformation, and implementing comprehensive strategic planning. Additionally, he leads an AI division at Benchmark Business Advisory, further emphasizing his commitment to leveraging modern technology for business growth.

Peter cares deeply about empowering business owners to build sustainable enterprises that are not overly dependent on any single individual or element. This approach is evident in his focus on digital experimentation, which underscores his belief in the importance of adapting to technological advancements. He also values thorough strategic planning as a means to prepare businesses for future challenges and opportunities, including potential exits with maximum value. His background, spanning from entrepreneurial ventures to advisory roles, indicates a passion for fostering business resilience and innovation through diverse and adaptable strategies.
