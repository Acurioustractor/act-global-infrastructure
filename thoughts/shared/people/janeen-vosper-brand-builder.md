## Understanding — drafted
Janeen Vosper is a seasoned professional with over 30 years of experience, operating under her business Speech Perfect. As a public speaking and professional presenting trainer, Janeen focuses on personal branding and communication skills to empower individuals and businesses. Her work includes guiding clients in crafting clear and impactful messages that help them stand out as industry experts and attract more clients. Through her training sessions, she aims to build the confidence of speakers who may be nervous or overlooked, transforming them into influential communicators.

Janeen cares deeply about helping others unlock their potential through effective communication and branding strategies. She is passionate about enabling individuals to articulate their unique value propositions and personal missions ("their why") with clarity and conviction. By doing so, she supports her clients in gaining the recognition they deserve within their respective fields. Her approach emphasizes not just technical speaking skills but also the psychological aspects of confidence building, which allows her clients to perform at their best when delivering powerful messages to their audiences.
