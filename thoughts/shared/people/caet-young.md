## Understanding — drafted
Caet Young serves as the Principal Advisor and Head of Media and Communications for the Minister for Housing, Public Works, and Youth in Queensland. Her role is pivotal in addressing the state's significant housing crisis by promoting stable living environments for young adults through initiatives like youth foyers, which provide supported living conditions for individuals aged 16 to 25 years old. Caet emphasizes long-term solutions and multi-annual funding to ensure that youth projects can effectively plan and sustain their services.

Caet seems deeply committed to the well-being of Queensland's youth, particularly focusing on housing stability as a cornerstone for addressing broader issues such as addiction and the impact of social media. Her work underscores the importance of creating environments where young adults can thrive and transition successfully into independent living. Through her efforts, she aims to empower young people by providing them with not just homes but supportive communities that foster personal growth and resilience.
