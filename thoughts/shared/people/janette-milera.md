## Understanding — drafted
Janette Milera serves as the Managing Director at Dreaming Connection and operates self-employed in her role as a Kaurna Narungga Arabunna Director. Over more than 15 years, she has dedicated herself to capturing the essence of South Australian Aboriginal communities through photography while also advocating for indigenous rights. Her work encompasses organizing significant protests and engaging in efforts to preserve land that holds cultural and spiritual importance for her community.

Janette cares deeply about preserving Aboriginal culture and heritage, particularly through the lens of visual storytelling and advocacy. She is actively involved in initiatives aimed at protecting sacred sites like Pirltawardli, which are crucial not only for their historical significance but also as tangible links to the Kaurna people's past. Additionally, her efforts extend to creating spaces where indigenous culture can be celebrated and shared, such as the creation of an indigenous garden at Lalor Tennis Club, highlighting her commitment to integrating cultural awareness into broader community settings.
