## Understanding — drafted
Bounces (Organization) focuses on enhancing employee well-being, engagement, and mental health support within organizations. They aim to foster a resilient workforce that is committed and satisfied by implementing comprehensive support systems. Through their work, Bounces seeks to drive transformative changes in organizational culture by ensuring employees receive the necessary care and resources for their personal and professional growth.

Bounces cares deeply about creating environments where individuals feel valued and supported. This organization emphasizes the importance of mental health and well-being as key components for employee success and organizational prosperity. They are particularly interested in leveraging hybrid models combining human coaching with artificial intelligence to provide more personalized and effective support systems. Additionally, Bounces is concerned with bridging the gap between research and practical application in organizational psychology, striving to ensure that theoretical advancements translate into real-world benefits for employees and organizations alike.
