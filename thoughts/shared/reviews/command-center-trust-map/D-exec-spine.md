# D — Executive spine (company, projects, ecosystem, strategy, today, business)

> Data-lineage audit of the command-center "executive spine" — the pages/routes meant to tell ACT what's happening across the company.
> Verified against shared DB `tednluwflfhxyucgwigh` 2026-05-26. Verdict legend per `_schema-truth.md` (🟢 real · 🟡 stale · 🟠 misleading · 🔴 broken · ⚫ not-wired · ⚠️ unverified).
> `/company` (`api/intelligence/route.ts`) findings reused verbatim from `2026-05-26-company-overview-data-audit.md`.

## Top findings

1. **Two finance engines disagree, and the worse one drives the headline page.** `api/intelligence` (the `/company` page) computes expenses with the sign bug (`total<0` → $0 expenses, transfers leak in as $1.4M "revenue"). The newer `v_project_financials` view and `api/business/overview` compute correctly by `type` (`RECEIVE`/`SPEND` with `abs`). So `/company` lies while the project pages are roughly right — same DB, opposite answers.
2. **The R&D "−26 days / Apr 30" alarm is hardcoded in TWO places and is wrong for ACT.** `api/intelligence` and `api/strategy` both hardcode `rdDeadline='2026-04-30'` (and strategy hardcodes `$407K` at stake). Per the locked R&DTI Path-C facts, 30 Apr 2026 does NOT apply to ACT (FY25-26 claim lodges Jul 2026–30 Apr 2027). Two exec surfaces show a false deadline alarm.
3. **`/compliance` is fully dead.** Reads `compliance_items` AND `tracked_documents` — both DEAD tables → throws → 500/empty. The business page's compliance section dodges this by rendering a hardcoded array baked into `api/business/overview`.
4. **Ecosystem site detail is half-dead.** `ecosystem/[slug]/health-history` + `/details` read `health_checks` (DEAD), `ecosystem/[slug]/deployments` reads `deployments` (DEAD). Site cards exist (7 real rows in `ecosystem_sites`) but uptime history and deploy history are always empty. `check`/`check-all` are pure placeholders.
5. **`/goals` is 100% mock data** (`const mockGoals = [...]`). No API, no DB, no `/api/goals` route. Pure UI fiction.
6. **`api/projects/financials` R&D block is silently always $0.** It queries `project_code='ACT-RD'` (0 rows — R&D is tagged on real project codes, not a synthetic ACT-RD) and groups by `vendor_name` (NOT a column on `xero_transactions`). Both silently null.
7. **`api/business/overview` cash + subscriptions are silently $0/null.** Reads `running_balance` (nonexistent col) → bankBalance/runway always null; filters subscriptions on `.eq('status','active')` but the real column is `account_status` → monthlySubscriptions always $0. P&L itself is correct, so the page looks half-alive.
8. **`api/business/upcoming` opportunities query is dead.** Filters `ghl_opportunities.close_date` — that column doesn't exist (real is `expected_close`) → the `.not('close_date','is',null)` predicate drops everything. Compliance dates are hardcoded with `currentYear` math that produces already-passed/nonsensical dates.
9. **Most-trustworthy surface is `api/strategy`** (despite its hardcoded R&D line) — it reads real, fresh tables/views (`project_monthly_financials`, `project_budgets`, `opportunities_unified`, `strategic_objectives`, `revenue_scenarios`, `sync_status`, `dext_forwarded_emails`) and computes burn/runway/coverage honestly. Its only dead pipe is `grant_financial_tracking` (DEAD → grantTracking always empty).
10. **Relationship/intelligence layer rests on a frozen table + a dead vote table.** `relationship_health` is STALE 27d (frozen since 2026-04-29); `api/intelligence/actions` also reads `insight_votes` (DEAD) and `contact_votes` (13 rows, last 2026-02-12) / `project_focus_areas` (stale 2026-02-27). Ranking still runs but on stale signal.

## What's salvageable

Make these the real executive view:

- **`api/strategy` (+ `/strategy` page)** — already the most honest, richest exec surface: real type-scoped finance, real burn/runway/coverage, real objectives/scenarios/pipeline. Two surgical fixes (remove the hardcoded `2026-04-30`/`$407K` R&D line; retire dead `grant_financial_tracking`) and refreshing `financial_snapshots` make it trustworthy. This — not `/company` — should be the org dashboard.
- **`api/ecosystem/pulse` and `v_project_financials`-backed routes** — the correct finance engine. Promote `v_project_financials` (or a shared `lib/finance.ts`) to be the *only* finance source so `/company` and `/business` stop disagreeing.
- **The health/observability trio** (`health/data-freshness`, `health/crons`, `ecosystem` site grid) — real, fresh, low-risk. Keep.

Archive / hide until rebuilt:

- **`/company` page** — demote/hide until it's rebuilt on the strategy/finance engine; it is the single most misleading surface (fake $1.4M revenue, false R&D alarm, 0/100 health).
- **`/goals`** (pure mock), **`api/business/balance-sheet`** + **`api/business/revenue-model`** (pure placeholders), **`api/compliance`** (dead tables), **`ecosystem/[slug]` deploy + uptime panels** (dead tables), **`ecosystem/check`/`check-all`** (stubs) — hide the buttons/cards or label "not wired" rather than rendering zeros that read as data.
- **`api/business/scoreboard` + `business/upcoming`** — repoint off the abandoned `fundraising_pipeline` and the nonexistent `close_date` column, or hide.
