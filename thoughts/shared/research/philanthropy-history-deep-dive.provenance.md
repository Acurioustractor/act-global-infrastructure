# Provenance: philanthropy-history-deep-dive.md

Companion to `philanthropy-history-deep-dive.md`. Documents sources, confidence per major claim, and gaps. Research date: 2026-05-26. Method: ~14 web searches plus 1 targeted fetch. No claim was written without at least one cited source; any claim that could not be sourced is marked [unverified] inline in the main file (none required it in the final draft).

## Confidence key
- Verified: stated directly by a credible source queried during research.
- Inferred: assembled or reasoned from sourced facts, not stated verbatim.
- Unverified: asserted without a confirming second source.
