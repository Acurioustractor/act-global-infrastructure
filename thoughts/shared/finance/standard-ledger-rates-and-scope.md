## Service lines

### Bookkeeping-Setup / Training-Bookkeeping
**For established businesses, they will:**
- Review any existing Xero setup
- Review any existing GST / PAYG / Superannuation / ATO obligations
- Review P&L and Balance Sheet accounts
- Review outstanding transactions
- Make any recommendations of adjustments / corrections — noting these may require additional cleanup / catch-up work / charges

**For all businesses, they will:**
- Implement a Standard Chart of Accounts, discussing revenue and expense requirements
- Guide you through the setup of Receipt Bank / Dext to send in invoices
- Provide **1 hour overview training** (via video conference): how their bookkeeping works, how they prepare the BAS, and how you work together to keep books up to date

During onboarding, if any additional cleanup / catch-up work is identified/agreed, they will **estimate the hours involved and confirm before proceeding**.

*Rates for any additional work: Team member $70/hr · Senior $85/hr · Team Lead $125/hr · Partner $450/hr*

### Bookkeeping-Launch
- Bookkeeping services **up to 100 transactions per month** — processing bank transactions, supplier invoices and expenses
  *(transaction volumes reviewed quarterly; may lead to a plan increase, subject to Fair Use Policy)*
- "Snap n Send" iPhone/Android app or email your payable invoices/receipts, with online storage
- **Quarterly Business Activity Statement (BAS)** submission

### Bookkeeping-Cleanup / Catch-up
- **Catch-up reconciliation:** Oct 2025 – Apr 2026 (**approximately 600 transactions**)
- **Estimated hours: 5–10 hrs**
- They prefer fixed price, but where there's insufficient information they may charge by the hour. Final hours confirmed and invoiced on completion. If work runs more than a month, they reserve the right to bill along the way (after communicating).

*Rates: Team member $75/hr · Senior $95/hr · Team Lead $135/hr · Partner $500/hr*

> **Cost flag on the catch-up:** at 5–10 hrs, a team-member-led job is ~**$375–$950 ex GST**; if a Senior/Team Lead does it, materially more ($475–$1,350+). Worth confirming *who* does it and a not-to-exceed before they proceed.
