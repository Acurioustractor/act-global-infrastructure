The four open decisions

Decision 1 · by 19 June

Which entity trades from 1 July?

  • A. Trade from the Pty as planned; convert to holdco later at a year boundary. Cost: a second round of novations later.
  • B. Incorporate ACT Projects now and trade from it; the Pty stays clean. Cost: new ABN, GST, bank, Xero in 18 days. Banking is the long pole.
  • C. Honest delay (Rule 2): sole trader keeps trading until the chosen entity is genuinely live. The safety net under A or B.
Ben + Nick, with Standard Ledger's view in writing. Everything in the table below moves with this call.
Decision 2 · questions before 26 June, answers before August

How does Butterfly connect?

  • The call sketched "wholly owned subsidiary". Butterfly is likely a company limited by guarantee: members, not shares. There is nothing to own in the share sense.
  • Holdco control pulls against the Indigenous-led board and the PBI + DGR standing that make Butterfly valuable.
  • Likely landing zone: beside the group, connected by arm's-length agreements. The DGR lawyer confirms or corrects.
Ben sends the question list before the 26 June handover.

Nick confirms; Standard Ledger advises treatment.
