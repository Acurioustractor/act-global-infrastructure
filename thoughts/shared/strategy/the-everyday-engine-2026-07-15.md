- 6:50am: field surfaces rebuild (warmth, owes, freshness canary).
- 7:40am: comms content calendar.
- Monday 7:45am: PPPP scan to Notion; Monday card generates.
- Overnight: night shift proposes enrichments and drafts; nothing external moves without a human.

### The road habit (this trip, from the phone)

1. **Morning, 2 minutes.** Open The Field morning read. Seven actions or fewer. It already shows who is warm-but-unasked, who is cooling, what ACT owes.
2. **Before any meeting.** Person page. Thirty seconds.
3. **After every yarn, one voice-note capture.** Who, where, what they gave, what you offered, what ACT now owes, next date. That single habit is the difference between this trip becoming history or vapour. The capture box and `field-captures.jsonl` already exist; they are in git, so the record is durable the moment it lands.

### The two switches that are built but off

- **Newsletter sends.** Four lists (act / goods / harvest / justicehub), consent stamped in code since June, drafters writing to `newsletter_drafts`, public archive live at act.place/newsletters. Zero editions ever sent. Audiences as of late May: 80 funders, 271 partners, 34 storytellers, 114 brand. Turning this on is an editorial decision and one afternoon.

This plus delivery feedback (which needs sends to exist) are the entire "communities interact" gap. Everything else about two-way is already principled into the system (replies always flow, community lane never dripped).
