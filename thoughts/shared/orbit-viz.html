What it says

The rings are Dunbar's layers (5/15/50/150) — a finite energy budget; more isn't better. They show where energy is going; the morning question is whether that's where it should go.

The warm core is small and funder-skewed; the doers are buried. Belonging-before-money exists to fix exactly this.

Only of the community's love is live. ACT owes consent conversations.

The line down the middle is the whole point: community is never laddered or dripped — it's owed back.

How to use it

Pick a project → the supporters who fit its needs light up. Start with the warmest. The constellation is your accountability list, not a target list.
