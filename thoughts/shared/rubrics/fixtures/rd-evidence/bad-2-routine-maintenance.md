# ACT-GD core activity: Dashboard improvements and monthly maintenance

## What we did

Throughout FY26 we performed routine maintenance on the Command Center finance dashboard, including:

- Fixing CSS issues across 12 finance pages
- Updating dependencies (Next.js 15.x, React 19, Tailwind 4)
- Adding new chart types as requested by stakeholders
- Patching minor bugs reported in the issue tracker
- Routine code refactoring for readability
- Staff training sessions for new contributors on the codebase

We also did some general business development work (writing pitch decks, preparing investor briefings) which we have included in this activity since it relates to communicating about Goods on Country.

## Goals

Our goal was to make the dashboard more reliable and easier for stakeholders to use. Marketing campaigns to drive engagement with the dashboard were also part of this work.

## Outcome

The dashboard is more stable. Stakeholders report it is easier to use. Customer acquisition for our pitch process improved. We trained 3 new staff on the codebase.
