# An ask to QBE Catalysing Impact

Dear QBE Catalysing Impact team,

Help us help young people who have been let down by every system meant to support them. Your generous backing of Goods on Country would change lives in ways that matter, and would mean the world to the communities we serve.

Goods on Country is doing remarkable work in some of the most challenging environments in regional Australia. Every day, the team turns up against the odds and creates real opportunities for First Nations young people who have nothing.

## What your gift would do

A contribution from QBE Catalysing Impact would let us continue this vital work. Your support would help us reach more young people, strengthen more communities, and build a more equitable future for Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander Australians.

We have not yet finalised a specific cost figure for the program. We will work that out together with QBE Catalysing Impact's team in the spirit of partnership. The important thing is the difference your investment will make to young lives.

## A heartfelt thank you

We know the demands on QBE Catalysing Impact are many. We are deeply grateful you would even consider supporting the work. The young people of Goods on Country deserve a chance, and QBE Catalysing Impact is the kind of partner who can give it to them.

## Closing

We would be honoured to share more about the program at your convenience. Looking forward to hearing from QBE Catalysing Impact.

With deep gratitude,
The ACT team
