# Mid-cycle update for Dusseldorp Forum, FY26 contained programme

Rachel and Jessica,

Dusseldorp Forum has been the education-led partner on the contained programme since the relationship opened. This is the mid-cycle update against the $16,500 received in FY26 (per signed letter wiki/funders/dusseldorp/2025-09-15-letter-of-support.md, paid INV-0214 on 2025-09-30).

## What the money has done

Three results that map directly to the education frame Dusseldorp invests in.

1. 18% of detained young people in the contained cohort completed at least one accredited education unit during FY26 Q1 to Q3 (data: ghl_contacts tagged 'contained-edu-complete' filtered by enrolment_quarter, 11 of 61 enrolled).
2. Young-people-as-experts: nine cohort members co-designed the FY26 BG Fit module curriculum (signed off by Toni-Maree Marshall on 2026-03-08, see wiki/projects/contained/bg-fit-curriculum-fy26.md).
3. Room 3 community-led delivery: every contained session in Q3 was delivered by community lead, not ACT staff (sign-in records in ghl_contacts.session_lead, 24 of 24 sessions).
