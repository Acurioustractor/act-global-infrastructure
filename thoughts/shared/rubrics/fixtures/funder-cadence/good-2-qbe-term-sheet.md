# Goods on Country investor brief for QBE Catalysing Impact

QBE Catalysing Impact backs scalable enterprises whose unit economics carry their own weight. Goods on Country is one of those: a First Nations enterprise where the per-cohort delivered cost is a known number, the handover is a binary, and the social-investment thesis lives or dies on the cost line, not the press release. The brief below assumes the cost report is signed and on the table.

## Unit economics

Per the cost report doc (wiki/projects/goods/cost-report-fy26.md, signed 2026-04-12 by both founders and Standard Ledger), the delivered cost per anchor cohort enrolled in Goods on Country in FY26 is $11,200 (signed cost report, line 47). The decision band sits between $9,800 (lean cohort) and $13,400 (high-touch cohort). The handover binary is the 90-day mark: at day 90 the cohort either runs without ACT staff hands on the table, or it does not.

## What QBE is being asked to back

A Year-1 outlay of AUD $400,000 against a delivered cohort target of 36 anchors (cost report line 91). The full 3-year envelope is AUD $1,180,000 if the Year-1 handover binary lands clean. The 3-year envelope is contingent on the cost ratio holding within the decision band.

## Why this is investor-grade

The numbers in the brief are not aspirational. They are the cost report's own outputs (signed contract reference: wiki/projects/goods/cost-report-fy26.md). The handover binary is recorded against each cohort by month 4 in the GHL pipeline (ghl_contacts.tag = 'goods-handover-tested'). The decision band has been pressure-tested against three control cohorts already through the Goods agent layer (BILL-2841, BILL-2879, BILL-2912 cover the agent infrastructure).

ACT runs the cost discipline through the agent layer (scripts/lib/goods-agent-runtime.mjs) so the unit economics are not a one-off. Every cohort posts a delivered-cost row to the ledger within 30 days of close.

## Closing

Three asks.

1. By 28 May 2026, QBE confirms the cost band Year 1 will be benchmarked against (the cost report is the proposed band; QBE may ratchet it down).
2. The term sheet is drafted by 11 June 2026 against that band.
3. First disbursement of $200,000 lands within 14 days of term-sheet signing, against the cohort cost-report signoff letter (wiki/projects/goods/cohort-letter-template.md).

Ready to walk through the cost report at any time in the next two weeks. The cost report doc is the authority; this brief is the room note.
