# Empathy Ledger for Organisations

**Every Story Matters. Yours included.**

## The Problem

Your organisation does extraordinary work — but proving impact shouldn't be this hard.

- **Annual reports take months.** Scrambling to reconstruct a year of work from memory, email threads, and scattered documents.
- **Stories get lost.** That powerful moment in the field? The Elder's words that changed everything? Gone — because nobody had time to write it down properly.
- **Community voices become statistics.** Funders want numbers. Communities want to be heard. You're stuck translating between the two.
- **Knowledge walks out the door.** When staff leave, their relationships, context, and institutional memory leave with them.

Sound familiar? You're not alone. Every community organisation we've spoken to describes the same pattern.

---

## The Solution

Empathy Ledger is a consent-first storytelling platform that helps organisations capture, preserve, and share impact stories — built on Indigenous data sovereignty principles (OCAP).

**Four modes. One platform.**

### CAPTURE — "Record As It Happens"
Voice notes, photos, meeting notes. Auto-transcribed and structured by AI. Stop losing stories to busy days. At year-end, you don't reconstruct from memory — the record built itself as you worked.

### STORY — "Look What We've Done"
Annual reports, impact summaries, photo essays, social content. AI assembles draft narratives from your captured content. You review and approve — the raw material is already there. Organisations using EL for 12 months don't *write* their annual report — they curate it.

### MEMORY — "Everything We Know"
Search across all your organisational knowledge via chat. "What programs have we run for young people?" "What did the board decide about X in March?" New board members get up to speed in one conversation. Grant writers find evidence in seconds.

### DIRECTION — "Where We're Going"
Define 3-5 aspirations. Projects and stories link to them automatically. Evidence surfaces as it's captured. Funders ask "what's your theory of change?" — the answer is live on screen, populated by real stories and real evidence.

## Cultural Safety — Built In, Not Bolted On

- **OCAP Principles** — Ownership, Control, Access, Possession baked into every feature
- **Elder Review Workflows** — Stories go through cultural review before publication (Standard 48h, Expedited 24h, Thorough 1 week)
- **Consent Management** — Ongoing consent with expiry, revocation rights, and granular access controls
- **Sacred Content Protection** — Multi-layer access controls for culturally sensitive material
- **Data Sovereignty** — Your data stays yours. Export everything, delete everything, any time.
