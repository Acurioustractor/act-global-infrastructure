## Thematic Overlaps: Where Catalysing Impact's Focus Areas Meet ACT's Work

### 1. Climate Resilience
- **Goods on Country** — IoT fleet telemetry (10 washing machines), regenerative supply chain, reducing environmental impact of laundry in remote communities
- **ACT Farm / Black Cockatoo Valley** — 40-acre Witta property, regenerative land management, IoT sensor network planned
- **The Harvest** — place-based events demonstrating land connection + cultural practice

### 2. Inclusion
- **Empathy Ledger** — community narrative sovereignty, OCAP-compliant, communities own their stories
- **JusticeHub** — justice system data transparency, making invisible inequities visible
- **GrantScope** — levelling the playing field for community-based orgs to access funding (Australia's 360Giving)

### 3. Social Enterprise Capability
- **ACT's dual-entity structure** — Foundation (charitable) + Ventures (mission-locked, 40% profit-sharing). CI values mission-lock.
- **GrantScope as commercial IP** — grant matching engine serving 14K+ grants, 9,874 foundations
- **EL + JH subscription model** — institutions pay, communities get free access (cross-subsidy)

### 4. Matched Funding Multiplier
- **R&D Tax Incentive** — 43.5% refundable offset on ~$468K eligible spend = ~$204K cash refund. This IS ACT's matching source.
- **Every CI dollar has a built-in match:** $1 CI → $0.44 R&D refund on the tech built with it
- **April 30 deadline** — R&D registration with AusIndustry. Timing: CI funds Year 1, R&D claim processes Year 2 = sustainable cycle.
