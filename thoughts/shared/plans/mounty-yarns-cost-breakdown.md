# Mounty Yarns — Just Reinvest Cost Breakdown

## Project Overview

A Curious Tractor delivered the Mounty Yarns backyard rejuvenation project across three phases in December 2025. The project involved the purchase and fit-out of a shipping container as a community activation space, along with site preparation, equipment hire, ground cover works, procurement of hardware and activation materials, and travel for two team members to deliver on-the-ground youth engagement.
