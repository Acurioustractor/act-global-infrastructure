## Context — why this plan exists

There was a 30 April deadline being raced that doesn't actually apply to ACT's situation. The unwind:

- **The 30 April 2026 deadline is for FY24-25 only** (year ended 30 June 2025). 10 months after the income year ends.
- **For FY25-26 (year ending 30 June 2026), the deadline is 30 April 2027** — eleven months from the date of this plan.
- **You can't lodge for FY25-26 yet anyway** — the income year hasn't ended. Registration happens AFTER 30 June 2026.
- The R&DTI portal at `incentives.business.gov.au` requires ABN to lodge. ACN alone doesn't work. There is no workaround for "lodge without ABN" — so the question of whether to lodge by 30 Apr is moot for our path.

**The honest framing:** there is no R&DTI action that must happen this week. The pressure right now is preparation — not lodgement. What matters for the FY25-26 claim is what we set up *between now and 30 June 2026*, so the Pty Ltd has a clean, well-evidenced claim by the time the lodgement window opens (Jul 2026 – Apr 2027).

Why FY24-25 is forfeited (per Path C decision):
- All FY24-25 trading was on Nic's sole trader ABN 21 591 780 066. Sole-trader R&D is excluded under ITAA 1997 s355-25.
- A Curious Tractor Pty Ltd didn't exist in FY24-25 (registered 2026-04-24), so it can't be the FY24-25 claimant.
- A Kind Tractor Ltd (CLG) is ACNC-registered and almost certainly income-tax-exempt → also excluded.
- Lodging anyway would be a defensible-eligibility risk + audit exposure + penalties. Not worth it.

## What's actually claimable for FY25-26

The FY25-26 income year is 1 Jul 2025 – 30 Jun 2026. A Curious Tractor Pty Ltd existed for the **last ~10 weeks** of that year (24 Apr – 30 Jun 2026).

**The eligibility rule:** the claimant must have *conducted* the R&D activities. Activities conducted by the sole trader before 24 Apr 2026 are not directly claimable by the Pty Ltd, because they were the sole trader's activities, not the Pty Ltd's.

**What that means in practice:**
- Direct expenditure by the Pty Ltd from 24 Apr 2026 onward → claimable (subject to all other rules)
- Director wages paid by the Pty Ltd from 24 Apr → R&D personnel costs (the $87K/yr leverage point in the existing plan)
- Continuing R&D projects post-cutover → the Pty Ltd needs to assume the activities, ideally via a documented IP/contract transfer from the sole trader (specialist call, see below)

**What this is NOT:** the existing $273K / $119K offset estimate in `rd-tax-incentive-comprehensive-report.md` for FY25-26 is overstated under this path because most of that spend pre-dates 24 Apr 2026 and was incurred by the sole trader. A revised conservative estimate is closer to **2 months of Pty Ltd spend + director wages + transferred-project costs from 24 Apr forward**. Specialist will refine.

The big year for R&DTI is **FY26-27** (1 Jul 2026 – 30 Jun 2027) — first full year under the Pty Ltd, full director wages, full $200K+ personnel claim possible.
