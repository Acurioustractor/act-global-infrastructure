## Decision (locked 2026-04-27)

Forfeit the FY24-25 R&D claim; claim **FY25-26 onward via A Curious Tractor Pty Ltd**.

- FY24-25 trading ran on Nic Marchesi's sole trader (ABN 21 591 780 066). Sole traders are NOT eligible R&D entities
  (ITAA 1997 s355-25), so FY24-25 is forfeited.
- The dormant charity (A Kind Tractor Ltd) is ACNC-registered and income-tax-exempt, so it is also excluded.
- A Curious Tractor Pty Ltd (registered 2026-04-24) is a company and therefore the eligible R&D entity. It is the
  claimant from FY25-26.

## Why (the reasoning)

The earlier plans (now archived: `_archive/2026-05/rd-tax-incentive-comprehensive-report.md` Feb 2026 and the
pty-ltd-transition Mar 2026) assumed a Pty Ltd in place from 1 Jul 2024 that did not exist. Lodging on that basis
would be a structural eligibility risk plus audit exposure plus penalties. Those plans are superseded.

## Timing

- The **30 Apr 2026** deadline applies to FY24-25 ONLY — not relevant to ACT.
- **FY25-26** is the first eligible year. Only activities conducted by the Pty from **24 Apr 2026** onward count
  (~10 weeks of the year). Lodge by **30 Apr 2027** (10 months after FY26 ends). Nothing is lodgeable until Jul 2026
  (the income year has not ended).
- **FY26-27** is the first full Pty year — the big year: full director wages, full-year R&D leverage. The World Tour
  Overseas Finding activity sits here.
