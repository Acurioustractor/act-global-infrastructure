## ⚠️ R&D caveat — read before relying on this for the refund
The original rationale included capturing Ben's **FY26 R&D contribution**. But Knight Photography is a **sole-trader/individual ABN**, and the recipient (Nic's sole trader) is also not a company — **neither is an eligible R&D entity**. Invoicing between them does **not** create a valid Pty R&D claim for this $250K. Whether any of this FY26 work can land in the Pty's R&D claim is exactly the **open SL question (#1)** — it likely can't for the pre-24-April period.

**So treat this pack as:** (a) clean cutover books, and (b) Ben's documented FY26 compensation. **Not** as a locked R&D lever until SL answers. Post-cutover (Phase 3, from 1 July 2026), Ben's R&D-eligible work flows through **A Curious Tractor Pty Ltd** payroll/contractor — that is the clean path, and it ties to the founder-personnel item in the SL question.
