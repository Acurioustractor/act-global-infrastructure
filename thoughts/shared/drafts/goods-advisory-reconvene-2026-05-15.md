### 3. The Mparntwe plant decision band

We are about to publish first delivered-cost numbers against the last 50 beds. The decision band is on the page:

- **Margin ≥25%** scale aggressively
- **Margin 15–24%** workable, identify cost-out levers
- **Margin 5–14%** red flag, current cost base does not support overhead

**Question for the group:**
