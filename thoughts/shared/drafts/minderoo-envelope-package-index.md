# The Minderoo envelope, what ships 1 May

A physical envelope and a live URL. Each piece designed to hold up alone. Read together, they are the pitch.

---

## Package architecture

Nine artefacts, three roles each plays:

| # | Artefact | Role | Form |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Cover letter** | opening · invites Lucy into Session 1 | A4 printed, signed |
| 2 | **Executive summary** | 1-page thesis · "Three Circles in 60 seconds" | A4 double-sided |
| 3 | **The Three Circles · Live dashboard** | proof, the system is real today · updates between sessions | QR + live URL |
| 4 | **Canonical pitch document** | the full argument · $2.9M, three years, what each dollar does | 16-page A4 booklet, bound |
| 5 | **Anchor Communities contact sheet** | **partners/relationships layer** · every named person reachable today | A4 double-sided |
| 6 | **Workshop proposal · Sessions 1 + 2** | the co-selection process for the remaining 2–3 anchors | A4 single-sided |
| 7 | **Brave Ones insert** | the daily practice · 3-site distributed cohort · 16 anchor voices | 8-page A5 zine |
| 8 | **Judges on Country one-pager** | 17 April 2026 field artefact · 90-second film + portraits | A4 double-sided |
| 9 | **Artefact 001 · Murri Watch** | physical sample of what Year 1 produces · collectible | small matte book |

**Dashboard's role in the package:** the live proof layer. Every other artefact is printed once. The dashboard updates between Sessions 1 and 2, each story approval, each outreach reply, each scoring refresh visible to Lucy in real time. **This is the "showing partners" element**, the audit trail that every name on the contact sheet is reachable and every anchor's consent is recorded.

## Three-year budget, where the $2.9M goes

The ask funds **editorial + relational work**, not platform build. JusticeHub, the Australian Living Map of Alternatives, Empathy Ledger v2 and CivicGraph are already built. Minderoo's $2.9M is what turns them from infrastructure into a national advocacy artefact.

## What each circle visibly produces

### Circle One outputs (the queryable layer)
- Australian Living Map of Alternatives, updated quarterly with community-voice layer
- CivicGraph, 100K+ organisations resolved with stories attached
- Empathy Ledger v2, monthly compilations ready for external publication
- Published service directory for magistrates (JusticeHub platform feature)

### Circle Two outputs (the relational practice)
- 7–8 anchor community partnerships with named governance records
- ~12 Brave Ones exchanges across 3 years (12+ young people travel)
- ~5–6 Diagrama / YOPE international trips (15+ young people see the overseas model)
- 3 annual Judges on Country convenings (165+ judge-touchpoints)
- Story corpus from each anchor (450+ consented stories by end of Year 3)

### Circle Three outputs (the released artefacts)
- 8+ case-study artefacts (Murri Watch is Artefact 001, shipping 1 May 2026)
- 25+ per-young-person journals (each a durable record of one young person's year)
- One documentary (Year 3 release)
- One national collection book
- One launch event (Year 3)

## How the envelope lands

**Session 0 (now, 1 May):** envelope arrives. Lucy reads it. Dashboard URL in her hand updates live.

**Session 1 (mid-May):** workshop. The five confirmed anchors are not discussed, Minderoo confirms them via the contact sheet. The session is about the remaining 2–3 anchor seats and the budget reality-check.

**Between Sessions 1 and 2 (late May):** ACT approaches Session 1-approved candidates. Dashboard reflects each outreach reply live.

**Session 2 (June):** confirmation. Budget locked. Year 1 commences 1 July 2026.
