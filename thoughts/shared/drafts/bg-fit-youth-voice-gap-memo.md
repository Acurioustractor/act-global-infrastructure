## Why this is not a weakness for Minderoo

The honest architecture of the campaign pool is:

- **Oonchiumpa carries the raw young-people voices** — Jackquann (14, Upper Camp), Nigel (14, Double Camp), Laquisha (16), Xavier — with direct quotes, named camps, age disclosed.
- **BG Fit carries the method-voice + one youth identity line** — Brodie (founder), Spinifex Residential group voice, Tuesday Gym Sessions, Rashad's *"everything"* on culture.

This is the right shape. Trying to force more BG Fit youth quotes than exist in the corpus would weaken the insert. The pitch uses Mparntwe for voice and Mount Isa for method-plus-travelability, and that's exactly what the Three Circles proposal needs to land.
