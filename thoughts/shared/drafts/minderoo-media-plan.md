## Part 4 — The consent ribbon

Every image and video on the envelope + dashboard needs a visible consent marker. This is central to Minderoo's language about "lived experience" and "communities closest to the challenges."

**Proposed convention:**
- Image caption: [who's in it, their mob] · [where taken] · [consent date, consent owner]
- Video caption: same, plus elder approval if applicable
- Metadata file alongside every asset: `<filename>.meta.md` with:
  - `subject_names`
  - `consent_tier` (public / community / elder)
  - `consent_owner` (named person + role)
  - `consent_date`
  - `governance_record` (path to wiki/decisions/ file)
  - `cultural_sensitivity_level`
  - `can_reproduce`: print / digital / both

This is the OCAP discipline applied to media. It is an audit trail the envelope itself can point at as proof-of-practice.
