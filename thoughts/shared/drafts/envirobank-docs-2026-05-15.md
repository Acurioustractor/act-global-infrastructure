## What Goods on Country is making with HDPE

A single product line that has scaled across remote Australia:

| Product | Status | HDPE per unit | Lifetime |
|---|---|---:|---|
| Stretch Bed | 389 deployed | 25 kg recycled HDPE | 10+ years |
| Washing machine fleet | 20 deployed | (HDPE in housing components only) | (steel-dominant) |
| Fridges | First requests in (Wilya Janta, Norm's community) | Design phase | TBD |

Total HDPE consumed to date: approximately 9.7 tonnes. Annual demand at current pilot scale: 4-5 tonnes. Annual demand at plant-scale (3,500 units in FY27-28 across two plants): 87-105 tonnes per year.

## What the Stretch Bed needs from the feedstock

- 25 kg of recycled HDPE per bed, post-sorting (we lose 15–20% to sorting waste)
- UV-stabilised
- Free of food-contact contamination
- Consistent melt index across batches (we are currently running on first-melt, not recycled-of-recycled)

## The "shining machine"

I think you are asking about the **industrial washing machine line** — that is the named product. Twenty units deployed. Aunty Dianne Stokes (Warumungu Elder, Tennant Creek) named the line in Warumungu language.

The machine is IoT-instrumented. Each unit reports cycle count, fault states, and ambient conditions back to our asset register. That telemetry feeds an R&D program on remote asset monitoring. The housing components are HDPE; the drum and motor are conventional white-goods stock.

If you meant something else under "shining machine", let me know.

## Where we want Envirobank in the picture

Envirobank as the supply-chain anchor for HDPE feedstock across both production sites:

- **Jinibara Country plant (Queensland)** — operational, 1,500 beds/year capacity, six FTE
- **Mparntwe plant (Arrernte Country, Alice Springs)** — site confirmed with Oonchiumpa Consultancy, build planned for FY27, target output 1,800 beds + washing machines

Indigenous-owned HDPE feedstock supplier into Indigenous-led manufacturing partnership is the loop we are trying to close. Envirobank fits that loop in a way no other supplier we have spoken to does.
