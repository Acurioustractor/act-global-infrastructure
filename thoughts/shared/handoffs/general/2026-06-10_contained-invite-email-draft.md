Hi {{FIRST_NAME}},

There is a shipping container arriving at Tandanya this June. From the outside it is steel and rivets and the smell of a working dock. Step inside and the first room closes around you the way a cell does, because that is what it is built to do.

Walk on and the container changes. The second room holds the therapeutic model David and the team at Diagrama have spent years shaping. The third holds something closer to home, the solutions communities are already running, in their own words, on their own Country.

We call it CONTAINED. Thirty minutes, three rooms, one question you cannot un-feel once you have stood in it: if we know what works, why do we keep building cells?

You are on this list because you have walked with us before. So this is an invitation, not a broadcast. Come and stand in it yourself, beside the Reintegration Puzzle gathering, the week of 24 June.
