# ACT as Philosophical Movement

**Captured:** 2026-01-12
**Source:** Ben's vision for where this is heading

---

## The Vision

ACT is not an organization. It's a **philosophical movement** that:

- Uses **art** as the primary medium for expressing truth
- Opens to **new technologies** (ALMA, story sovereignty, AI) as tools for liberation
- Draws from **stories from very long ago** - truth-telling traditions
- Works **closely with artists** - being artists ourselves
- Pushes past the **normal** into ACT's normal, into Witta's normal, into the world's normal outside of limitation

---

## Every Project Has a Reflection Space

For every ACT project, there should be:

### 1. A Way to Express It as Art
- Not just reports and metrics
- Visual, auditory, experiential expression
- The story of what happened, told beautifully

### 2. A Way for Community to Add Their Voice
- Not just ACT telling the story
- Community participants contributing their perspectives
- Living document that grows with input

### 3. A Way to See the Impact
- ALMA signals made visible
- Evidence of transformation
- Connection to the communities it came from

## The Community Story

ACT's story is not written by ACT alone. It's crafted through:

### Monthly Dinners
- People helping craft the community story
- Shared meals, shared reflection
- Art emerging from conversation

### Workshops
- When we host, we also listen
- Participants become co-authors
- Methods become movements

### Connection Points
- When people want a place to connect
- The Harvest bringing groups together
- Community seeking community

### The Harvest
- Markets as gathering
- Groups looking for community, revenue, work, anything
- Economic activity as art form
