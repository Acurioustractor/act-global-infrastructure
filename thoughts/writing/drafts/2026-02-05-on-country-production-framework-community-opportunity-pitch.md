# On-Country Production Framework - Community Opportunity Pitch

#  On-Country  Production: Pathways for Community Opportunity

## The Vision
A community-driven manufacturing initiative that creates meaningful employment and skill development for young people in Central Australia and Townsville, breaking cycles of disadvantage through purposeful production.

## What We're Building
### Bed Manufacturing Ecosystem
- **Local Production**: Handcrafted beds designed and manufactured on-site
- **Skills Development**: Comprehensive training program for young people
- **Community Ownership**: Production model that returns economic agency to local communities

## Opportunity Highlights
- **Jobs Creation**: Direct pathways for young people to learn trade skills
- **Economic Sovereignty**: Locally-controlled production model
- **Skill Progression**: From entry-level manufacturing to advanced production roles

## Our Commitment
- Authentic community engagement
- Transparent impact measurement
- Prioritizing community perspectives over external metrics

## Next Steps
- Prototype development
- Community consultation
- Skills training framework design

*This is more than a production line. This is a pathway to opportunity.*
