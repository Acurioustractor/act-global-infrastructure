# Minderoo Pitch Package — The Three Circles

## Core ask

- **Primary ask:** **$2.9M over 3 years**
- **Clean Year 1 entry point:** **$780K**
- **Current public cohort:** **4 confirmed anchors + 3 to be co-selected with Minderoo (= 7 total)**
- **Opening noun:** **community**, not platform

## The one-sentence ask

> Help turn the evidence Minderoo already supported into a living, Indigenous-led mechanism for recognition, redirection, and community-led proof.

## The arithmetic line

> Minderoo has helped Australia name the cost of acting too late. The Three Circles is a chance to fund what Australia should be investing in instead.

## Policy bridge

### Verified COLI claims

Use only these public-facing figures unless a richer claim is re-verified from source:

- Australia spends **$22.3B** each year on late intervention
- up from **$15.2B** in 2019
- **child protection = $10.2B / 43%**
- **youth crime = $3.4B / 14%**

### Approved phrasing

- **Primary phrase:** `early recognition`
- **Bridge sentence:** `Early recognition is how this proposal translates Minderoo's early-intervention logic into the lives of young people the system has already started to misrecognise.`
- **Anchor lines:**
  - `Minderoo has named the cost of acting too late.`
  - `The Brave Ones is early recognition for young people the system has already started to misrecognise.`
  - `From the kettle to the capital.`

### Approved institutional critique tone

Say the hard thing in board-safe language:

- Australia’s late-intervention economy is highly organised.
- The community-led prevention and recognition economy is not.
- The people keeping children connected to culture, family, school, and Country are doing life-saving work with almost no structural support, visibility, or decision-making power.

### Banned claims and banned frames

Do not use:

- unverified downstream justice dollar figures as headline claims
- `fund our youth justice program`
- `fund our platform`
- `fund our AI`
- stale legacy western-Sydney cohort references in Minderoo-facing material
- outdated certainty about a fixed ten-community cohort

## The current spine

1. **COLI is the hard policy doorway.**
Minderoo already understands the national cost of late intervention.

2. **The Third Reality is the method doorway.**
Data, evidence, funding context, and sovereign story are usually separated across different rooms.

3. **The Brave Ones is the living response.**
It gives the work a body: journals, portraits, exchanges, books, exhibitions, and public proof.

4. **The Three Circles is the envelope.**
The ask holds the communities, the evidence spine, and the outward-facing artefact together.

## Money story

| Bucket | 3-year total | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| **Partner / place-based hold** | **$1.4M** | Community relationships, local coordination, travel, protocol, and the time required to stay |
| **Generate evidence** | **$800K** | JusticeHub, CivicGraph, Empathy Ledger, documentation, consent workflow, journals, portraits, and traceability |
| **Develop + advocate** | **$700K** | Editorial production, public artefacts, exchanges, and outward-facing civic argument |

## What stays central

- the people already doing the right thing every day with culture, relationship, and almost no structural support are the heroes
- the platform matters only because it makes their work visible and harder to dismiss
- the artefact matters only because it carries their truth into rooms they are normally excluded from
- the money matters only if it shifts power toward the communities holding the work
