# ACT Studio

> *The art and innovation banner. Where the experimental work lives — and where the things ACT learns publicly first take physical form.*

## Where this sits in the ecosystem

- **CONTAINED sibling:** [[contained]] is the studio's largest piece — the touring shipping container youth justice exhibition. CONTAINED has its own narrative project at `wiki/narrative/contained/` because it is the campaign layer; the rest of the studio sits in Tractorpedia as reference.
- **The Brave Ones** is the studio's photographic art companion to CONTAINED — it now lives under [[justicehub|the JusticeHub cluster]] because it's also the visual architecture of the JusticeHub data layer
- **LCAA position:** every studio piece is the **Art** phase of the [[lcaa-method|Listen → Curiosity → Action → Art]] loop — the form that makes a methodology walkable
- **Innovation lab:** the studio is where ACT tries things first — most of what becomes a program (Goods on Country, JusticeHub, Empathy Ledger) had a studio prototype before it had a budget

## Why "Studio" is the right banner for all this

The user's framing: *"the studio is all the innovation and other projects into one banner."*

The studio is **not a narrative project** of its own — it is the parent cluster for everything ACT makes that does not yet belong to a single funded program. When a studio piece grows into a program (CONTAINED → JusticeHub campaign layer; The Brave Ones → JusticeHub visual layer), it gets cross-linked into the relevant program's cluster but its file stays here as the origin record.

## Status

Active cluster. Multiple pieces at different stages (Caravan: born Feb 2026; Confessional: established; Vagina: coming soon; Treacher: ideation). Not currently in the narrative store as its own project. Will gain a `wiki/narrative/act-studio/` folder when the Studio becomes a pitch for Creative Australia, Australia Council, or a corporate art-and-innovation funder like Atlassian Foundation.

## Cross-project work the studio touches

- **CONTAINED** — the Studio's flagship and the campaign layer of JusticeHub
- **Empathy Ledger** — every Studio piece tests the consent + sovereignty platform in a different format
- **Goods on Country** — the maker culture in cars-and-microcontrollers feeds the dignified-essential-goods design discipline
- **The Harvest / ACT Farm** — Regional Arts Fellowship is where the studio meets the farm

Future work: a single Studio funder pitch (Creative Australia, Australia Council, Atlassian Foundation, Arts NSW) would be assembled from this cluster the way the Minderoo pitch was assembled from the JusticeHub cluster.
