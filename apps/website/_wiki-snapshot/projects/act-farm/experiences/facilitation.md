# Facilitation for your team

> We facilitate. That's the offer. Your meeting, your offsite, your strategy day — on our ground or yours, with ACT holding the room.

**Status:** Active | **Code:** ACT-FM-EXP-facilitation | **Cluster:** [[act-farm|ACT Farm]]

## What it is

We facilitate other people's work. Not ours dressed up as yours. You bring the group, the stakes, and the decisions that need making. We bring the design, the craft, and the discipline to hold the room so the group can actually move.

Our facilitation comes out of years of convening in community, capital, justice, and storytelling contexts — most visibly in [[community-capital|Community Capital]], where we hold the room while others hold the money. Same method applies to your board, your exec team, your partnership negotiation, or your co-design day.

## Who it's for

- Exec teams needing a facilitated strategy day or offsite
- Boards running a hard conversation that needs a third party in the chair
- Cross-organisation partnerships designing how they'll actually work together
- Community cohorts doing co-design with institutional partners
- Funders running grantee convenings who want someone else holding the space

## What happens

- Discovery call: what's the decision you need, who's in the room, what's the tension
- Design: we send a session architecture for you to sign off on
- Facilitation: we run it
- Debrief: a short written note of decisions and moves, not a deck

Can run at [[black-cockatoo-valley|BCV]], at your venue, at [[the-harvest|The Harvest]] in Witta, or virtually. If the stakes are high, we almost always recommend somewhere that isn't your usual room.

## LCAA Phase

In the [[lcaa-method|LCAA Method]], facilitation is where ACT applies Listen → Curiosity → Action → Art as session architecture in someone else's room. Here's how the four phases show up across a facilitated engagement:

- **Listen** — discovery call to surface the actual decision the room needs, who's in it, and what tension is real
- **Curiosity** — session design that names the question rather than the agenda; ACT sends the architecture back for sign-off before anything is built
- **Action** — the facilitation itself; ACT in the chair, the group doing the work, decisions named in the room
- **Art** — a short written note of decisions and moves — not a deck — as the durable record

ACT facilitates other people's work. Not ours dressed up as yours.
