# ACT Farm Experiences

> The catalog of ways people come to [[black-cockatoo-valley|Black Cockatoo Valley]] — stays, residencies, retreats, facilitated sessions, health intensives, maker weekends, and harvest pairings with [[the-harvest|The Harvest]] in Witta.

**Cluster:** [[act-farm|ACT Farm]] | **Place:** [[black-cockatoo-valley|Black Cockatoo Valley]] (Jinibara Country, QLD) | **Sister hub:** [[the-harvest|The Harvest]] (Witta)

## How to read this index

Each entry links to a canonical experience page written in the first person — ACT describing, in its own voice, what it offers. Case studies (evidence of what this work has already produced) sit in the sibling [[case-studies/README|case-studies]] folder.

Experience pages follow a shared shape: what it is · who it's for · what happens · price and duration · ecosystem links · how to inquire. The ACT Farm website composes these into `/experiences/<slug>` pages and routes inquiries to the right GHL pipeline.

## Categories

### Stays

Accommodation on Country with light-touch hosting.

- [[stays|Stays at Black Cockatoo Valley]] — Pink Cabin, Train Carriage, and the direct-booking path for self-guided time on Country.

### Residencies

Longer placements for artists, practitioners, and researchers working on regenerative, creative, and storytelling practice.

- [[artist-residency|Artist Residency at BCV]] — artists placed on Country for extended creative practice, with ACT as host and curatorial partner.
- [[industry-residency-at-the-harvest|Industry Residency at The Harvest]] — extended placements where artists, designers and ACT work alongside a regional industry and its people. Sibling to the artist residency, at the Witta site.
- [[empathy-ledger-storytelling-residency|Empathy Ledger Storytelling Residency]] — embedded storytelling practice feeding the [[empathy-ledger|Empathy Ledger]].

### Retreats

Structured convenings for teams, cohorts, and partner groups that want to do serious work on Country.

- [[the-space-at-bcv|The Space at BCV]] — the retreat and convening space itself, available to groups who want to hold their own work here. See [[case-studies/community-capital-at-bcv|Community Capital at BCV]] for a live example of how it's been used.
- [[corporate-innovation-retreats|Corporate Innovation Retreats]] — facilitated multi-day retreats for leadership teams and strategy groups.

### Facilitated sessions

ACT-led work where the facilitation is the product, not the room.

- [[innovation-sessions|Innovation Sessions]] — focused sessions across youth justice, AI and systems, data and evidence, and storytelling practice.
- [[facilitation|Facilitation for your team]] — ACT facilitating your meeting, offsite, or strategy day on our ground or yours.
- [[workshops-at-bcv|Workshops at BCV]] — a rolling program of short-form workshops on the land.

### Health

Therapeutic and health-led intensives that use Country as the treatment environment.

- [[junes-patch-healthcare-retreat|June's Patch Healthcare Retreat]] — health-focused retreat program grounded in the [[junes-patch|June's Patch]] practice.
- [[dad-lab-at-bcv|DAD.LAB at BCV]] — men's and fathers' health intensives run in partnership with [[dad-lab-25|Dad.Lab.25]].

### Makers

Making as a way of being on Country — food, craft, and shared work.

- [[makers-weekend|Goods on Country Makers' Weekend]] — hands-on making weekends in partnership with [[goods-on-country|Goods on Country]].

### Harvest pairings

Pairings with [[the-harvest|The Harvest]] in Witta — shared meals, paired workshops, seasonal programs across the two sites.

- [[harvest-pairings|Harvest Pairings]] — the BCV ↔ Witta program strand, including monthly dinners and paired workshop series.
