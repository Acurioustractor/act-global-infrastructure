# A Curious Tractor (ACT)

> Not a charity, not a consultancy, not an NGO. A regenerative innovation ecosystem that powers communities without owning what it builds.

> Identity sits downstream of [[soul|Soul]]. If anything in this file contradicts soul, soul is right.

## What ACT Is

A Curious Tractor (ACT) is a social enterprise co-founded by [[../people/benjamin-knight|Benjamin Knight]] and [[../people/nicholas-marchesi|Nicholas Marchesi OAM]]. The name comes from the agricultural PTO (Power Take-Off) mechanism, the connection shaft that transfers engine power from a tractor to attached implements. A tractor powers things. The farmer directs it. When the season ends, it's unhitched.

This is not a brand metaphor. It is a delivery philosophy: ACT builds platforms, tools, and programs *with* communities, designed to be handed over and run without ACT involvement. The measure of success is not reach or scale. It is that ACT becomes unnecessary.

ACT partners with First Nations communities and is not a First Nations organisation.

## Entity Structure

ACT operates through a small group of entities, each with a clear job. The point is not bureaucracy. The point is legibility: each project's economic story stays its own, instead of mashing into a single ledger no one can read.

| Entity | Role |
|--------|------|
| **A Curious Tractor Pty Ltd** | The trade muscle. Owned 50/50 by Knight Family Trust and Marchesi Family Trust. Service revenue, contracts, and corporate-trustee grants land here. Distributions flow through the trusts to the founders. |
| **Harvest Pty Ltd** (forming) | Standalone P&L for The Harvest. Earns its own. Grows its own. |
| **Farm Pty Ltd** (forming) | Standalone P&L for ACT Farm. Conservation, land practice, residencies, BCV. |
| **A Kind Tractor Ltd** | Company Limited by Guarantee. Currently dormant, not yet DGR-endorsed. The public-good cap, ready to activate when the time comes. |

A sole trader entity (Nic's ABN) is the legacy trading vehicle, winding down by 30 June 2026.

Trading sits in the Pty Ltds. Charitable work sits in A Kind Tractor when it activates. Each project lives in its own ledger. Mission stays independent of commercial pressure because no single entity has to be everything.

For canonical entity detail (ACNs, ABNs, accountant, bank), see [[../decisions/act-core-facts|ACT Core Facts]]. For how money flows through these entities and into the four lanes, see [[four-lanes|The Four Lanes]].

## The LCAA Method

ACT's operating methodology is [[lcaa-method|LCAA]]: Listen, Curiosity, Action, Art. It is not a linear project management framework. It is a practice loop. Art returns you to Listen. The cycle continues.

Every ACT project, relationship, and piece of infrastructure can be tagged with where it sits in the cycle. The discipline is staying present to where you actually are rather than rushing to action before the listening is done.

## Beautiful Obsolescence

[[beautiful-obsolescence|Beautiful Obsolescence]] is ACT's core design discipline: building toward its own irrelevance. Every platform, tool, and engagement is designed with handover in mind from day one. Sunset clauses in agreements, documentation as a deliverable, training embedded in delivery, open-source codebases: all expressions of the same commitment.

> "If we cannot hand it over, we are still in Curiosity."

The test for any ACT project: can the community run this without us? Can they modify it? Can they export their data? If any answer is no, the work is not finished.

## How ACT Differs from a Typical NGO

| Typical NGO | ACT |
|-------------|-----|
| Builds programs it maintains indefinitely | Designs for handover from day one |
| Measures reach, headcount, outputs | Measures community ownership and capacity |
| Extracts impact data for funders | Builds data sovereignty for communities |
| Grows its own footprint | Shrinks its own footprint |
| Separate "research" and "delivery" teams | LCAA integrates them: listening IS the work |

ACT's financial sustainability comes from trading revenue, not from perpetual grant dependency. The structure exists to preserve mission independence.

## The Ecosystem of Projects

ACT runs a cluster of interconnected projects spanning place, technology, justice, and art. They are not siloed programs. They are components of a single ecosystem:

- **Empathy Ledger:** the connective tissue. Sovereign storytelling that gives communities ownership of their narratives.
- **JusticeHub + CivicGraph:** the evidence layer. Data and stories combined into the [[third-reality|Third Reality]].
- **The Harvest + ACT Farm + Black Cockatoo Valley:** the Place cluster. Regenerative land practice and community hubs.
- **PICC:** the proof case. 20 years of community control on Palm Island showing what's possible.
- **The Studio layer** (CONTAINED, The Confessional, Uncle Allan, Gold.Phone): Art as the fourth LCAA phase, the highest expression of the work.

See [[act-ecosystem|ACT Ecosystem]] for the full map.

## Where ACT Operates

ACT is headquartered in Queensland, with active work across:

- **Palm Island (Bwgcolman):** PICC partnership, Empathy Ledger field work
- **Witta, QLD:** The Harvest regenerative hub
- **Mount Isa (Kalkadoon Country):** BG Fit, CAMPFIRE, youth justice
- **Alice Springs / Central Australia:** Oonchiumpa, Barkly Backbone
