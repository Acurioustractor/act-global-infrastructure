## Core Principle

> Country Sets the Pace

The land is the teacher and the limiter. We do not scale beyond land capacity or community readiness. Conservation-first is the baseline.

## How Land Practice Shapes Every Project

Every ACT project connects back to land:

- **[[goods-on-country|Goods on Country]]** — Manufacturing grounded in place and local materials
- **[[empathy-ledger|Empathy Ledger]]** — Stories from and about Country
- **[[justicehub|JusticeHub]]** — On-Country healing as an alternative to detention
- **[[the-harvest|The Harvest]]** — Enterprise that funds land care
- **[[act-studio|ACT Studio]]** — Technology serving conservation goals

## Jinibara Country

ACT operates on Jinibara Country. This shapes:
- How we relate to the land
- Our responsibility to Traditional Owners
- Our approach to cultural protocols
- Our commitment to co-stewardship
