The opportunity cost Minderoo has already named

Australia spent $22.3 billion last year on children and young people in crisis.

A 47% real-terms rise since 2019. Child protection is the largest line ($10.2B, 43%). Youth crime is the second ($3.4B, 14%). Over half of children in Victoria's youth detention came via child protection. They are not two problems. They are one trajectory — the same children, five years apart.
