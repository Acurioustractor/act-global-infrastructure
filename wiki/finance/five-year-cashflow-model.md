## Key Assumptions

- ACT Pty Ltd is established FY26 Q4 with family trusts for both founders
- 40% community profit share applies to net profit after all costs (salary, ops, R&D)
- Minderoo STAY lands at Crescendo tier ($3.43M/3yr); fallback to Foundation tier ($1.8M/3yr) reduces Flow by ~$350K/yr
- CivicGraph MRR assumes 40–50% YoY growth from $400K base; exit multiple 3–6x ARR
- Ground revenue assumes Act-Farm and Harvest are a single operational unit by Y2
- Other grants assumed to decline as earned revenue grows (grant dependence drops from 40% Y1 to 15% Y5)
- Founder salary is a cost (deducted before profit and before the 40% community share is calculated)
- R&D Tax Incentive at 43.5% refundable offset, annual claim, ~4–10 week ATO turnaround after lodgement
