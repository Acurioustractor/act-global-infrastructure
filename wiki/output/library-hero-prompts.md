## LOCKED — JusticeHub colophon (the JH mark)

The single most important brand element. Used on every cover, slipcase, journal, and travel diary in the series. **Always identical. Always sepia. Always with the word JUSTICEHUB legible.**

**Locked file (transparent PNG):** `wiki/output/justicehub-logo.png`
**Source flat graphic (white bg):** `wiki/output/justicehub-logo-source.png`

**Locked prompt fragment to paste into every cover/asset prompt:**

> A small sepia-brown circular rubber-stamp colophon, about the size of a fingernail: a thick circular band with the word "JUSTICEHUB" in small serif caps along the top inner curve, a thin arc of seven small dots along the bottom inner curve, and a bold serif "JH" monogram in the centre. Slightly imperfect rubber-stamp character with subtle ink fades. NO year, NO date, NO numbers. NO Penguin colophon. NO other branding marks.

**Where it goes:**
- On the seven STAY Series cover books → bottom centre, just above the series mark
- On the slipcase spines → at the foot of each spine
- On the Travel Diary → bottom of the front cover
- On any new asset in the family → same place, same size

## Locked visual language

- **Paper:** aged cream / off-white with subtle foxing and faint corner wear
- **Ink:** dark sepia-brown
- **Accent:** warm red (used sparingly — small dots, a single linocut accent)
- **Type:** classic serif caps for titles, italic serif for subtitles, small caps for series marks
- **Series mark:** `THE STAY SERIES · NO. 01` through `NO. 07`
- **Colophon:** small linocut **"JH" monogram** inside a sepia-brown circle (about the size of a fingernail), at the foot of every spine. NO Penguin colophon. NO other branding.
- **Photography:** real photograph aesthetic, soft natural daylight from upper left, real shadow, real paper depth. NOT digital, NOT vector.
- **Surface:** soft cream linen for flat shots, real wood shelf for the bookshelf shot
