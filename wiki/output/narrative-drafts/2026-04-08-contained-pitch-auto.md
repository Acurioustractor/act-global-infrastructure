*File:* [`claim-unit-economics-must-be-real.md`](../../narrative/contained/claim-unit-economics-must-be-real.md) · *Frame:* structural · *Times deployed:* 1

### 2. Claim: Community ownership is binary, dated, and signed

*File:* [`claim-handover-is-the-test.md`](../../narrative/contained/claim-handover-is-the-test.md) · *Frame:* structural · *Times deployed:* 1

**The argument:** "Community-led" is the most abused phrase in social enterprise. Goods on Country has converted it into four binary tests at month 6: physical control of the factory, named operational lead on payroll, the community entity invoicing the buyer, ≥50% of production community-controlled. *Partial counts as NO unless there's a written 30-day path to YES.* Either it happened or it didn't.

**What we have already said (do NOT repeat verbatim):**

- "Yes / no, no weasel 'partial'"
- "PARTIAL counts as NO unless there is a written, dated path to full YES within 30 days"
- "This document is the difference between 'we hope handover happens' and 'we have dated tests for whether it is happening.'"
- "4/4 = scale, 3/4 = fix, 2/4 = stop, ≤1/4 = redesign"
