# Wait, you built all of this?

CONTAINED is a shipping container with three rooms.

That is the part you can see.

What you cannot see, when you walk through it, is the four-layer infrastructure underneath that makes the rooms work — and that has been getting built quietly for the last three years while no one was paying attention.

Layer one is **CONTAINED.** The container. The rooms. The thirty minutes you spend inside. This is the part that makes you feel something.

Layer two is **JusticeHub** — the public evidence platform at justicehub.com.au. 1,775 verified community-led models from across Australia. Indexed, mapped, citable. Six live AI agent tools. The platform is the answer to the question *"okay, so what works instead?"* — and it is already running.

Layer three is **Empathy Ledger.** The consent-and-sovereignty layer that holds 2,569 voice transcripts from 342 storytellers across 18 partner organisations. Every quote is traceable. Every storyteller can revoke. OCAP and CARE protocols at the data layer, not aspirational. This is the part that lets a young person at Oonchiumpa speak in their own voice and have that voice cited inside the JusticeHub evidence base — without losing control of it.

Layer four is **CivicGraph.** The legibility layer the hierarchy refused to build. 100,036 entities. 199,001 relationships. 94.1% entity-match accuracy. Daily syncs from public sources. This is the layer that lets capital, magistrates, and the public see the work without going through the hierarchy.

The four layers share a database. They reference one another by foreign key. When a young person at PICC speaks into Empathy Ledger, the quote can be cited inside JusticeHub, mapped against the funding flows in CivicGraph, and used as evidence in the next round of ALMA verification. **This is the rare social-venture stack where the listening, the doing, and the money-and-power all share a database.** Nobody else in Australia has this.

We are not building it. We have built it.

Across the seven projects in the A Curious Tractor ecosystem, we have earned **$1.75 million in revenue in the last fourteen months.** This is not pre-revenue. This is not a hobby. This is a working venture that has been talking about itself like a hobby because no one taught us how to do it the other way.

CONTAINED launches in Mount Druitt on April 20. The shipping container is the front door. The rest of the building is on the inside.

If you have been watching this campaign and wondering *"who actually built this and how does it sit on top of anything"* — the answer is: two people, with our hands, on top of three years of infrastructure that already runs.

The evidence layer is real. The story layer is real. The legibility layer is real. The campaign layer is the room you walk into.

Then you walk through it.

— Ben
