# 21x

If you are an Aboriginal or Torres Strait Islander child in Australia, you are 21 times more likely to end up in youth detention than your non-Indigenous classmate.

That is not a metric. That is the system.

It has been the system since 1991, when the Royal Commission into Aboriginal Deaths in Custody made 339 recommendations and the youth justice ones were the first ignored.

It has been the system through every Closing the Gap report since 2009. The number is in every one of them. The number gets read aloud in Parliament every year. The number does not move.

The kids being detained today were not born when the Royal Commission was written.

CONTAINED launches in Mount Druitt on April 20. It is a shipping container. It contains nothing new — only what every report since 1991 has already said. The reason we built it is that nobody in the country can defend the 21 times figure when it is in the room with them.

We will be in the room with it from April 20.
