# Output

> Reports, audits, and query results generated *from* the wiki — not part of the wiki's curated knowledge, but artifacts the wiki produced for humans.

Examples of what lives here:

- `url-audit-YYYY-MM-DD.md` — weekly GitHub + Vercel verification reports (from `scripts/wiki-verify-urls.mjs`)
- `lint-YYYY-MM-DD.md` — weekly wiki health reports (from `scripts/wiki-lint.mjs`)
- `query-YYYY-MM-DD-<slug>.md` — one-off query results that weren't worth promoting to a synthesis page

The distinction:

- **synthesis/** is *knowledge that compounds* — it gets cross-linked, queried, and built on
- **output/** is *evidence the wiki worked* — historical reports, dated, append-only, not part of the article graph
