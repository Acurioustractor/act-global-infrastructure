# The Edge Is Where the Healing Is — JusticeHub as the world model for community-led justice

## Answer

## The argument in one sentence

Authority-at-the-edge is not new — it's been documented by Tyson Yunkaporta on this continent, by Elinor Ostrom across five continents, and by Fundación Diagrama at national scale in Spain — and JusticeHub is the world model that lets community-led justice compound across Australia the way the hierarchy has refused to.

## The proof points that survive scrutiny

1. **Bourke / Maranguka** — KPMG-evaluated. 23% violent crime drop, 38% major offences drop, 42% fewer custody days, ROI $3.10:$1. Town-scale, community-led, hard data. Canonical Australian case.
2. **Oonchiumpa (Mparntwe)** — MS story: identity restoration is the mechanism, not behaviour management. 95% diversion, 97.6% lower cost. Quote from Tanya Turner: *"knowing who you are, knowing where you come from, knowing who your family is."*
3. **Fundación Diagrama (Spain)** — 30+ centres, 30 years, reoffending 20–30% vs Australia's 85%. Government-funded, community-run. Answers the "does it scale?" question with three decades of national-scale evidence.

Notably **dropped**: BG Fit (single program, not a scalable model). Confit Pathways (replaced by Maranguka which is more rigorously evaluated and geographically consistent).
