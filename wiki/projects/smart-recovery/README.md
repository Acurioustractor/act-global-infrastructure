# SMART Recovery

> Self Management And Recovery Training — peer-led addiction recovery infrastructure.

The SMART program family is a cluster of three related initiatives: peer connection, healthcare provider integration, and the GP toolkit. They share a methodology and an audience but each one targets a different layer of the addiction recovery system.
