# SMART Recovery GP Kits

> Practical toolkits for GPs to introduce SMART Recovery to patients in primary care settings — meeting people at the point of first contact with the health system.

**Status:** Active | **Code:** ACT-SM | **Tier:** Satellite

## Overview

SMART Recovery GP Kits are a healthcare integration initiative designed to bring evidence-based addiction recovery support into the primary care encounter. GPs are often the first — and sometimes only — professional contact for people experiencing addiction, yet most GP practices lack structured tools to introduce peer-based recovery pathways in a consultation.

The GP Kits address this gap directly: equipping general practitioners with accessible, ready-to-use resources that open the conversation about SMART Recovery and create a pathway from the GP's office to community-based peer support.

## Purpose and Rationale

The primary care system sees people at their most vulnerable and most accessible moments. A person who will never seek out an addiction service may still see their GP. GP Kits are designed to leverage this touchpoint — turning a routine consultation into a potential entry point to recovery.

SMART Recovery's evidence base makes it particularly suited to the GP context: it is science-based, secular, and self-empowerment focused rather than reliant on a spiritual or disease-based model that may not fit all patients.

## Key Features

### Primary Care Optimised
Resources are designed for the time constraints, patient literacy diversity, and clinical communication norms of general practice — not adapted from specialist addiction settings.

### Practical and Ready-to-Use
Kits provide GPs with materials they can hand to patients, scripts for introducing the topic, and clear pathways to local SMART Recovery meetings or digital resources.

### Healthcare Integration
The project works within existing Medicare and primary care structures rather than creating parallel systems, making adoption lower-friction for busy practices.

## Relationship to SMART Recovery Ecosystem

GP Kits are one part of a three-project SMART Recovery ecosystem within ACT's portfolio:

- [[smart-connect|SMART Connect]] — the digital platform for peer support and community connection
- [[smart-hcp-gp-uplift|SMART HCP GP Uplift]] — workforce upskilling for healthcare providers, complementing and extending the GP Kits work

Where GP Kits focus on tools and pathways, HCP GP Uplift focuses on building GP confidence and competence in addiction conversations.
