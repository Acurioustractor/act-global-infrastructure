# PICC — Palm Island Community Company

> Twenty years of community control on Bwgcolman Country.

The parent project is [[picc|PICC]] — a community-controlled organisation with 197 staff delivering services across North Queensland with a 78% diversion rate. The other files in this folder are sub-projects, infrastructure, and accountability artefacts that all sit under PICC's umbrella.

## System Position

This cluster is the durable memory for the PICC field.

- the wiki holds the canonical framing of PICC, its sub-projects, and why Palm Island matters in the ACT ecosystem
- [[empathy-ledger|Empathy Ledger]] holds the live corpus: stories, photos, portraits, storm documentation, and editorial curation
- the websites should compose those layers into public project pages, proof surfaces, and future PICC-facing hub surfaces

This matters especially here because PICC is both one of the strongest proof points in the whole ecosystem and one of the places where sovereignty, consent, and cultural protocol matter most. The split is defined in [[wiki-project-and-work-sync-contract|Wiki Project & Work Sync Contract]] and [[living-website-operating-system|Living Website Operating System]].
