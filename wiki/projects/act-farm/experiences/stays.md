# Stays at Black Cockatoo Valley

> Two cabins, 150 acres of Jinibara Country, and room to be quiet. The Pink Cabin and the Train Carriage are bookable directly. This is the self-guided end of the farm — you're on the land, we're light-touch hosts.

**Status:** Active | **Code:** ACT-FM-EXP-stays | **Cluster:** [[act-farm|ACT Farm]]

## What it is

We offer direct-book accommodation at [[black-cockatoo-valley|Black Cockatoo Valley]] — the Pink Cabin and the Train Carriage. Unlike the rest of the experiences in this catalog, stays are instant-book, not inquiry-based. You pick your dates, pay the deposit, and we confirm.

Stays are deliberately simple. You're not signing up for a program. You're renting a cabin on a working land-practice and spending time with the place. We ask you to respect conservation boundaries we explain on arrival and otherwise leave you alone.

## Who it's for

- Writers, artists, and practitioners wanting quiet work time
- Couples and small families wanting a weekend on Country
- Locals from the hinterland who want a night away without the drive
- People scoping a longer engagement (residency, retreat) who want to see the place first

## What happens

Not much, by design. You arrive, we do a brief walk-around, and then the cabin is yours. Linen, kitchen basics, firewood in season. Optional: garden access, walking tracks, and — if you're here at the right time — a [[the-harvest|Harvest]] breakfast in Witta on the weekend.

We don't run a program around stays. If you want more — a workshop, a residency, a retreat — we have other offers for that.
