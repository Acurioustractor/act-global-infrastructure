# The Space at BCV

> We hold a convening space at Black Cockatoo Valley — the kind of room where serious conversations about community, capital, and country can actually happen. [[case-studies/community-capital-at-bcv|Community Capital has already taken a year here]]. Other groups can too.

**Status:** Active | **Code:** ACT-FM-EXP-the-space-at-bcv | **Cluster:** [[act-farm|ACT Farm]]

## What it is

We offer the retreat and convening infrastructure at [[black-cockatoo-valley|Black Cockatoo Valley]] as a space for other organisations to do their own work in. Accommodation, gathering spaces, shared meals, land access — the same environment we use to run ACT's own retreats is available to groups we align with.

This is not venue hire. We hold the space in the ACT way: conservation-first, relational, unhurried. Groups that come here know they're convening on Jinibara Country, inside an active land-practice, not in a generic retreat centre. That's the point.

[[case-studies/community-capital-at-bcv|Community Capital]] has paid for a year of accommodation here. [[the-caravan|The Caravan]] lives here too. The retreat came home. Other groups can hold their own retreats, cohort convenings, and leadership intensives in the same space — with or without ACT facilitation.

## Who it's for

- Community-sector organisations wanting serious time off-site with their teams or cohorts
- Funders and impact-investing groups who want to meet community leaders somewhere that signals weight
- Strategic partners running leadership development, peer councils, or capital-adjacent convenings
- Small high-trust groups (12–25) who want a room that doesn't feel like a conference hotel

We're not the right space for high-volume events, extractive programming, or groups whose purpose is at odds with conservation-first land practice.

## What happens

The shape of a convening here depends on the group. We can provide:

- Accommodation across our cabins, rooms, and supported overflow
- Gathering spaces — inside, outside, around fire, at table
- Shared meals with [[the-harvest|Harvest]] pairings where the season allows
- Optional ACT facilitation, or you run your own agenda with us as hosts
- Optional Country orientation and land-practice time
- Time with [[the-caravan|the Caravan]] as a gathering object when it fits

Groups typically use the space for 2–5 nights. We block other activity on the land during convenings to protect the quiet.

## How to inquire

This is an inquiry-based offer. Tell us about the group, the purpose of the convening, rough dates, and how much or little you want us to hold. We reply within a few working days and run a short call before confirming. Inquiries go through `/experiences/the-space-at-bcv` on the ACT Farm site.
