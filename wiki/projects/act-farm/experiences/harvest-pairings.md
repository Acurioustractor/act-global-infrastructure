# Harvest Pairings

> [[the-harvest|The Harvest]] in Witta and [[black-cockatoo-valley|BCV]] up the hill are a pair, not two venues. Monthly dinners, paired workshops, shared CSA, and seasonal programs run across both sites.

**Status:** Active | **Code:** ACT-FM-EXP-harvest-pairings | **Cluster:** [[act-farm|ACT Farm]]

## What it is

Harvest Pairings is the program strand that runs between [[the-harvest|The Harvest]] — the regenerative community hub on the former Green Harvest site in Witta — and [[black-cockatoo-valley|Black Cockatoo Valley]], the 150-acre land holding up the road. They're both on Jinibara Country, operated by the same ecosystem, and the food and program loop between them is deliberate.

Some things happen at BCV because the land makes them possible. Some things happen at Harvest because the kitchen and the public-facing hours make them possible. Pairings are the format that uses both.

## Who it's for

- Locals from Witta and the Sunshine Coast hinterland
- Guests staying at BCV who want the Witta side of the experience
- Groups booking [[experiences/the-space-at-bcv|The Space at BCV]] who want catered meals and workshop days off-site
- Partner organisations running programs that want both a working venue (Harvest) and a residential layer (BCV)

## What happens

The pairings we run:

### Monthly dinners at BCV
A rotating monthly dinner at Black Cockatoo Valley using Harvest kitchen and produce — small, seasonal, open to a short wait-list. Not a restaurant, not a pop-up; a table.

### Paired workshops
Some workshops split across the two sites — for example, a preserving workshop where the food is grown at BCV and preserved in the Harvest kitchen, or a pottery series that fires in Witta and glazes at BCV. See the current calendar on `/experiences/workshops-at-bcv` and the Witta side on theharvestwitta.com.au.

### CSA (Community Supported Agriculture)
Seasonal produce shares, run out of [[the-harvest|The Harvest]], using produce from BCV, Witta Farm, and partner farms. Sign up via The Harvest site.

### Retreat meal extensions
Groups booking BCV retreats or [[experiences/the-space-at-bcv|The Space at BCV]] can add Harvest-catered meals, a kitchen day at Harvest, or a workshop series that uses both sites.
