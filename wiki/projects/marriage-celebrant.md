# Marriage Celebrant

> Ben Knight's civil marriage celebrant practice, connecting ceremony with community.

**Status:** Active | **Code:** ACT-CB | **Tier:** Satellite

## Overview

Marriage Celebrant is Ben Knight's registered civil marriage celebrant practice. Operating as a satellite project within the [[act-ecosystem|ACT ecosystem]], it brings the same care for storytelling and human connection that runs through ACT's other work into the ceremony space.

The practice reflects ACT's broader philosophy that meaningful rituals and community gathering are acts of social infrastructure. Each ceremony is approached as a story worth telling well, with attention to the couple's own language, culture, and context rather than templated scripts.
