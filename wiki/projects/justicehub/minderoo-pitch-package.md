## The Crescendo tier — detailed budget breakdown ($3.43M / 3 years)

| Category | Year 1 | Year 2 | Year 3 | 3-year total | % of total |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Team** (1 FTE lead + 1 support, 4/8 cadence, cultural review fees as line items) | $260K | $300K | $320K | **$880K** | 25.7% |
| **Community partnerships** (10 anchor communities, fees, travel, cultural protocol fees, cohort costs) | $280K | $350K | $370K | **$1,000K** | 29.2% |
| **The library production** (7 STAY Series books, ~50 young-person journals, 10 community volumes, the national edition, hand-stitched binding, design, printing) | $80K | $200K | $420K | **$700K** | 20.4% |
| **International leg** (Year 2 European exchange — Diagrama, YOPE Holland, Children's Hearings Scotland — ~14 travellers, reciprocal European visits) | $30K | $400K | $40K | **$470K** | 13.7% |
| **Empathy Ledger studio** (consent infrastructure, transcription pipeline, photo + handwriting + video production, asset management) | $60K | $80K | $80K | **$220K** | 6.4% |
| **Documentation + comms** (Substack, social, the launches, the publishing-platform side of the library) | $30K | $50K | $80K | **$160K** | 4.7% |
| **Evaluation + reporting** (formative + summative; KPMG-grade if Minderoo wants it) | — | — | — | (folded into team + community lines) | — |
| **Approximate total** | **~$740K** | **~$1,380K** | **~$1,310K** | **~$3,430K** | 100% |

**Year 1 is intentionally lighter** because Year 1 is Ember + Tinder (the Fire Crescendo methodology). You cannot light the bonfire in Year 1. The budget shape mirrors the methodology shape.

**Year 2 is the heaviest** because Year 2 includes the European exchange (~$400K of the international leg lands in Year 2) AND the kindling-to-campfire stage of the in-community work.

**Year 3 is the library year** — the production and printing of the seven method books, the ten community volumes, the national edition, and the launches. The library line item ($420K in Year 3) is the largest single line in the entire budget because the library is the destination.

### Foundation tier — $1.8M / 3 years (the fallback)

If Crescendo is too much, the Foundation tier runs 5 communities instead of 10, defers the European exchange to one scoping trip, simplifies the journal binding, and produces 5 community volumes instead of 10. **Foundation = 46% of the cost of detaining one child for three years.**

### Bonfire tier — $6.0M / 3 years (the dream)

Bonfire adds: 2 FTE + 1 support team (instead of 1 + 1), the full European exchange with reciprocal visits and a full cohort week per host country, three chapter deliveries of journals (Y1/Y2/Y3 instead of just Y3), slipcase boxed sets in every State Library, and a national gallery tour through NGA / MCA / NGV / regional galleries. **Bonfire = 154% of the cost of detaining one child for three years.**
