# The Harvest

> *Cultural Preservation. Sustainable Farming. Intergenerational Learning. Witta, Sunshine Coast hinterland.*

The Harvest is the program. Green Harvest Witta is the property. Both files belong together because the program lives on the property and the property is the program's defining infrastructure.

## System Position

This folder is the durable memory of the Harvest cluster.

- the wiki explains the relationship between the public-facing hub, the property, and the broader place cluster
- [[empathy-ledger|Empathy Ledger]] carries live story, seasonal media, and editorial proof
- the websites compose those layers into `/harvest`, project pages, articles, and community invitations

That split matters because The Harvest should feel alive without rewriting its truth in three different systems. The field contract is in [[wiki-project-and-work-sync-contract|Wiki Project & Work Sync Contract]] and [[living-website-operating-system|Living Website Operating System]].
