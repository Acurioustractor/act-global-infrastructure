# SMART HCP GP Uplift

> A healthcare provider education initiative — building GP and allied health confidence and competence in supporting patients through addiction recovery.

**Status:** Active | **Code:** ACT-SM | **Tier:** Satellite

## Overview

SMART HCP GP Uplift (Healthcare Provider GP Uplift) is the workforce development arm of ACT's SMART Recovery ecosystem. Where [[smart-recovery-gp-kits|GP Kits]] provide practical tools for the consultation, HCP Uplift works at the level of the clinician — building the knowledge, skills, and confidence GPs and healthcare providers need to have more effective addiction support conversations.

The project recognises a structural gap: healthcare providers receive limited training in addiction medicine, and even less in peer-based, non-12-step recovery models like SMART Recovery. When clinicians don't know the evidence base, don't feel confident raising the topic, or default to referral-only approaches, patients miss recovery support they could benefit from.

## Key Focus Areas

### Provider Education
Training GPs and allied health providers in the SMART Recovery model — its evidence base, its approach, and how it differs from and complements other pathways.

### Addiction Support Capability
Building the clinical skills to screen for addiction issues, hold non-judgmental recovery-oriented conversations, and navigate the complexity of co-occurring conditions.

### Healthcare System Integration
Working within CPD (Continuing Professional Development) structures and primary care education channels to embed SMART Recovery awareness into how healthcare providers learn and practice.

## Relationship to GP Kits

HCP Uplift and [[smart-recovery-gp-kits|GP Kits]] are designed as complementary:

- GP Kits give clinicians the practical resources
- HCP Uplift builds the capability to use them well

A clinician with high capability but no tools is limited. A clinician with tools but low confidence won't use them. The two projects together address both barriers.

## SMART Recovery Ecosystem

This project sits within a three-part ACT SMART Recovery portfolio:

- [[smart-connect|SMART Connect]] — digital peer support platform
- [[smart-recovery-gp-kits|SMART Recovery GP Kits]] — primary care toolkit
- **SMART HCP GP Uplift** — workforce capability development

## LCAA Phase

**Action** — Training delivered, providers upskilled, system-level capability built. The knowledge and tools are now in the hands of the clinicians who can use them.
