# SMART Connect

> A digital connection platform integrating peer support, digital access, and community connection — bringing innovation, operations, and storytelling together in one ecosystem.

**Status:** Active | **Code:** ACT-SM | **Tier:** Satellite

## Overview

SMART Connect is a digital platform project that sits within the broader SMART Recovery ecosystem ACT has been involved in supporting. It brings together three strands that are often siloed in recovery and community health settings: the operational infrastructure of running a program, the peer support relationships that make recovery sustainable, and the storytelling that builds community and reduces stigma.

The platform is designed to close the gap between people who need peer support and the community networks that can provide it — while also addressing the digital access barriers that disproportionately affect people in recovery or experiencing disadvantage.

## Key Areas

### Peer Support
Connecting people in recovery or at risk with trained peer supporters who have lived experience. SMART Connect creates structured pathways for these relationships rather than relying on informal, ad hoc connections.

### Digital Access
Recognition that digital exclusion compounds social disadvantage. SMART Connect addresses this not only by building accessible technology but by supporting people to develop the skills and access needed to engage with it.

### Community Connection
Beyond one-to-one peer support, the platform fosters broader community belonging — the network effects that reduce isolation and build resilience over time.

### Integration of Innovation, Operations, and Storytelling
SMART Connect is notable for deliberately integrating these three layers. Innovation without operational continuity fails communities. Operations without storytelling fails to inspire or sustain. Storytelling without innovation stagnates. The platform holds all three.

## Relationship to SMART Recovery Ecosystem

SMART Connect is closely related to two other ACT-supported SMART Recovery projects:

- [[smart-recovery-gp-kits|SMART Recovery GP Kits]] — equipping GPs to introduce SMART Recovery tools in primary care
- [[smart-hcp-gp-uplift|SMART HCP GP Uplift]] — upskilling healthcare providers in addiction support

Together, these three projects represent a system-level approach to addiction recovery: platform connection (SMART Connect), primary care entry point (GP Kits), and clinical workforce capability (HCP Uplift).

## LCAA Phase

**Action** — SMART Connect is a direct delivery project: building the platform, connecting the people, closing the gaps. The listening and design phases inform it, but SMART Connect is where that work becomes infrastructure.
