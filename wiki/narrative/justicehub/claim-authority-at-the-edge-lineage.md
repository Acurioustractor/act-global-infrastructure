# Claim: Authority-at-the-edge is not new. The infrastructure to make it legible is.

**The argument:** This idea is not new. It is the synthesis of four lines of evidence that have all been on the table for decades. **Tyson Yunkaporta** *(Sand Talk, 2019)* — Aboriginal thinking is a structural refusal of hierarchy. Songlines as a working world model that has run for 65,000 years. Authority sits with the people closest to the consequence. **Elinor Ostrom** *(Governing the Commons, 1990; Nobel 2009)* — distributed governance outperforms hierarchy where authority sits with the people closest to the consequence and they can see each other's solutions. **Fundación Diagrama** *(Spain, 30+ centres, 30 years)* — national-scale community-based youth justice with 13.6% reoffending vs Australia's 85%. Government-funded, community-run. The proof that this scales. **Edith Henderson Grotberg** *(Lima 2006)* — international resilience research across twenty countries: I HAVE / I AM / I CAN. Resilience is contagious. What was always missing was the legibility infrastructure — the layer that lets capital, magistrates and the public see the work without going through the hierarchy. **We finally built it. It's called CivicGraph. The library is the proof.**

## What we haven't said yet

- Never said as **the closing line of a pitch** — the most quotable line in the entire STAY program is the lineage paragraph; it has not been the closing slide of any deck
- Never paired with **Australian proof points alongside the international ones** — Maranguka (23% violent crime reduction) and Oonchiumpa (95% diversion) deserve to be in the lineage table
- Never said as **a refutation of "we need more research"** — *"Yunkaporta has 65,000 years of data. Ostrom won a Nobel. Diagrama has 30 years. Grotberg has 20 countries. The research is done."*
- Never positioned as the **structural critique of Australian academia** — *"Most Australian youth justice research re-derives, in English, from a centre, what Yunkaporta has been writing in language for decades. Stop commissioning the re-derivation."*
- Never used as the **opening of a funder meeting** — *"You are about to fund a methodology that was proven nationally in Spain in 1991, in Lima in 2006, and on this continent for 65,000 years. We did the engineering."*
