# Claim: Minister Laura Gerber MP claimed $560M for 22 First Nations-led organisations and has not named them

## Sentence for copy

*"On 14 April 2026, Queensland Minister Laura Gerber MP said the government is investing $560 million in 22 First Nations-led organisations delivering 28 new services. She has not named them. As of 15 April, the list is not public."*

## The force-a-trap payload

This is the load-bearing claim across three campaigns. It is designed to put a minister on a fork where both public answers advance the campaign:

**If Murri Watch is one of the 22:** the language of "cancellation" was wrong and the re-investment story needs to explain why re-investment looks like closure.

**If Murri Watch is not one of the 22:** the minister owes a name for who will sit in the watch-house from 1 July.

**Either answer exists. Neither has been made public.**

## The fact that keeps this claim live

The silence itself. Every day the list stays unpublished, the claim gets stronger. This is the point of tracking silences in `_shared/orgs.md` as evidence — silence is the most publicly available form of state behaviour, and the force-a-trap gives it weight.

## What could disprove it

- Minister publishes the list of 22 organisations → force-a-trap collapses to a single fork ("is Murri Watch on it?"), and the claim retires or transforms
- Minister publishes a partial list → claim narrows to the unnamed remainder
- The $560M figure is corrected → wait for the correction and redeploy with the correct figure
