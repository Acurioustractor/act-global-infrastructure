# Claim: Stories from Inside — the first cross-jurisdictional youth-justice listening project

**The argument:** 49 youth-justice / detention quotes already in the corpus. Add international prison sites — Diagrama School Uganda, Holland's closed-prison case studies, Spanish Diagrama centres, possibly Lesotho — and you have **the first cross-jurisdictional youth-justice listening project that includes both incarcerated young people AND the community programs trying to keep them out.** Fundable as a sub-project. The contacts are already in the world tour data.

## What we haven't said yet

- **Never said as a sub-project pitch** to a funder — *"This isn't just CONTAINED. It is a multi-country listening project that uses the same consent infrastructure across five jurisdictions."*
- Never connected to **CONTAINED Room 1 (designed by young people)** — Room 1 is the AU node of a five-country Stories from Inside network
- Never positioned as the **research arm** of JusticeHub — the corpus is the dataset that justifies the policy ask
- Never used to recruit **a co-funder consortium** — Diagrama, JCF, an Australian foundation, a UK or Dutch human rights funder
- Never said in **the kids' voices** — the strongest version of this claim is a Diagrama young person speaking next to a Banksia Hill young person
- Never connected to the **Holland prison closure** as a media hook — *"The Dutch closed prisons because they ran out of inmates. We have 380 quotes from Australian kids who would not have been inmates in the Dutch system."*
