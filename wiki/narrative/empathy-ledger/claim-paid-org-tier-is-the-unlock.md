# Claim: 18 orgs on the platform. Zero paying. The first one is the unlock.

**The argument:** Empathy Ledger has 18 orgs running on it. None of them are paying. Most have budgets. PICC, Orange Sky, SMART, Snow Foundation, June Canavan, Diagrama — these are not mission-critical-poor. A standard org tier ($249–$499/mo × 8 paying orgs) = $24K–$48K MRR (~$300K–$575K ARR). The first paying org is the unlock for everything downstream — Grantscope freemium, white-label licensing, the methodology consulting flywheel.

## What we haven't said yet

- **Never said publicly.** The platform's marketing does not include a price.
- Never said to PICC or Orange Sky directly — *"What we have built for you for free is now a product. Here is what we charge for it."*
- Never paired with the **'free flagship case study' offer** — the first org gets a discount in exchange for a written reference
- Never connected to the **white-label tier** path — funders who fund the SaaS tier for their grantee portfolio
- Never used to **defend the ongoing engineering investment** — *"This is now a product line, not just internal infrastructure."*
- Never said: *"Most of the 18 orgs would pay if asked. Nobody has asked."*
