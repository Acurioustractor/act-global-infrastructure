# Claim: Each chapter is a question, not a destination

**The argument:** The world tour's chapters are not places — they are quests. Witta: *"Do these stories matter at all?"* Alice Springs: *"Do they hold up away from the people who built it?"* Lesotho (if real): *"What does justice look like in a country where prisons are not the answer?"* Holland: *"Why do their kids come home?"* Each chapter ends with the question that forces the next.

## What we haven't said yet

- Never said in the **public website copy** — the existing world tour page has poetry but the chapter-as-question structure has not been explicitly named
- Never used as a **funder hook** — *"We are not pitching you a trip. We are pitching you 16 questions and the field research to answer them."*
- Never said as a **case-file structure** — each chapter publishes as: question on arrival, the people, the field journal, the artefacts, the surprise, the question on departure
- Never paired with the **~20 publishable artefacts = monetisation** path
- Never said: *"Polished is the lie. Friction is the story."*
