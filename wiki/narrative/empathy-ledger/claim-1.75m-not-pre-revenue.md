# Claim: $1.75M in 14 months. Not pre-revenue. Under-narrated.

**The argument:** ACT has earned $1.75M across the ecosystem in 14 months. The pitch language has been treating it like a hobby. The truth is: we are running a working venture that does not yet know how to talk about itself. The world tour's commercial job is to make the next $5M obvious to the people who have it.

## Stat watch

- **$1.75M / 14 months** — sourced to ACT Global Infrastructure repo per synthesis. Verify against actual Xero / accounting data before using publicly. Should be a clean number with a date stamp before it goes in any deck.
- The synthesis projects **$700K – $1M additional in next 12 months from existing assets**, no new products. Pair this with the existing $1.75M to make the total picture: ~$2.45M – $2.75M earned in the first 26 months of operation.

## What we haven't said yet

- **Never said as a funder pitch line.** The existing decks treat ACT like an early-stage social venture asking for runway. It is not.
- Never said as a **monthly run-rate** — what is $1.75M / 14 months in monthly terms? ($125K/mo). That is the number a funder needs to see.
- Never said: *"We are not asking you to fund our existence. We are asking you to compound a $125K/month working venture."*
- Never used to **disqualify the 'social enterprise needs philanthropy' frame** — most funders assume social ventures lose money. The data says otherwise.
- Never paired with the **revenue-by-project breakdown** that would show which streams produced the $1.75M
- Never said as a **brag** because the user has been culturally trained not to. The brag is the product.
