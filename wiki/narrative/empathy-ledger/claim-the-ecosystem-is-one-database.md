# Claim: One database. Listening, doing, money. Nobody else has this in Australia.

**The argument:** Most Australian social ventures have a CRM (or a spreadsheet), a separate impact measurement system (or none), and a funder pipeline that lives in someone's email. ACT runs Empathy Ledger (listening), CivicGraph + JusticeHub (doing-evidence), Grantscope + ACT Global Infrastructure (money-and-power) on a connected stack with shared identifiers. That structural advantage is the entire reason the loop works — and it is uniquely Australian.
