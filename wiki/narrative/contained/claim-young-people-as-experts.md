# Claim: The people who survived the system are the ones redesigning it

**The argument:** Room 1 of CONTAINED was designed by Isaiah, who lived inside detention. He knows the steel benches, the mesh windows, the bolted furniture, the institutional logic that strips everything portable. He now works at Interlace Advisory. The young people are not case studies. They are the experts. That is the whole argument — not a footnote.
