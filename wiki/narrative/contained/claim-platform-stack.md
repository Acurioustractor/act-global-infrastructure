# Claim: Four layers, one system

**The argument:** CONTAINED is not a one-off art piece. It sits on top of a stack: **CONTAINED** (the emotional front door), **JusticeHub** (the public evidence and coordination layer), **Empathy Ledger** (consent, story, community authority), **ALMA** (the evidence intelligence engine that keeps it current). Each layer makes the others legible. Together they are infrastructure for a movement, not a campaign with an end date.

## What we haven't said yet

- **Never said publicly on social.** This is the largest gap in the entire system. The public sees CONTAINED. The funders see the stack. The first time most public viewers will learn that ALMA, EL, and JH exist together is if we tell them.
- Never said as a **"wait, you built all this?"** post — the underestimation moment is itself the argument. Two people. Four products. Working in concert.
- Never said as a **diagram post** — one image with the four layers, one sentence
- Never used to argue **against single-issue campaigning** — *"This is what it takes to actually shift a system. Not a hashtag. A stack."*
- Never positioned as **public infrastructure** — the comparison to ABS, AIHW, or the Productivity Commission, except built by two people without a budget
- Never said: *"Most campaigns are a single layer. We built four because the problem is four layers deep."*
- Never made the **JusticeHub-as-the-real-product** point — *"CONTAINED is the door. JusticeHub is the room you walk into when you decide to act."*
