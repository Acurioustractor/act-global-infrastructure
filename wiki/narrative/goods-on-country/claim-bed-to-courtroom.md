# Claim: From a bed to a courtroom is a line you can draw with your finger

**The argument:** One in three children in remote communities has scabies at any given time. Scabies leads to Rheumatic Heart Disease. 59% of remote homes lack washing machines. Sick kids don't go to school. Out-of-school kids end up in the system. The bed Goods on Country builds is a youth justice intervention. The pipeline is direct, and it starts at the ground.
