# Claim: Not charity. Enterprise.

**The argument:** Goods on Country is not a donation program. Communities collect waste, make products, run the business, and own the entity that invoices the buyer. The bed is the product. The factory is the proof. The handover is the test. Anyone framing this as a charity case has not understood the structure.
