# The Buttery

> The Buttery is a drug and alcohol rehabilitation and treatment service based in Bangalow, northern New South Wales. Founded in 1976, it is one of Australia's longest-running therapeutic communities.

**Relationship to ACT:** The Buttery is one of four Y1 bespoke customers for [[empathy-ledger|Empathy Ledger]]'s Annual Field Service (alongside JCF, [[picc|PICC]], and SMART Recovery), at roughly $50K annual engagement.

## ACT Work in Progress

- **Fifty Years of Coming Home** -- 200-page hardcover anthology documenting The Buttery's 50-year history, interviews, archival photos, Paul Kelly connection, timeline, program data, staff and alumni voices
- **Cinematic documentary** -- The Buttery's 50-year cinematic documentary, already in build, screening at capital campaign launch event
