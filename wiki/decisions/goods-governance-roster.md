## The line that must never blur

These people are **co-owners and governors**, not supporters on a journey. The Butterfly board is
the Indigenous-majority governance of the Goods charity and DGR home. They are sovereign, they hold
the keys by right, and they are at the deepest belonging from the first moment. **They are never
laddered, funnelled, scored, or shown as "leads."** The supporter membership ladder (see
[[act-belonging-model|The ACT Belonging Model]]) is for funders, members, buyers and partners, and it
applies to none of the people on this roster. Any in-app surface must keep this roster structurally
separate from the supporter ladder. (Brand voice: no em-dashes in ACT-facing copy; Indigenous place
names first.)

## Context

Goods on Country's charity and DGR home is **The Butterfly Movement Ltd** (CLG charity, ex-TABOO
Foundation; ACN 155 132 684; ABN 22 155 132 684). It has held Item 1 DGR and PBI endorsement since
17 January 2012. An Indigenous-majority board is being installed, with the stewardship handover on
**26 June 2026**. See [[act-core-facts|ACT Core Facts]] and [[act-business-architecture|ACT Business
Architecture]].
