# CONTAINED tour — campaign brief

## What

A physical shipping container that surfaces local funding and evidence data and collects stories. Currently based at Mountie Yarns (NSW). Touring across 8 confirmed locations. Each stop produces claim files, photographs, and storyteller content with EL v2 consent.

## Why

The Centre (in the Three Circles proposal) is digital. CONTAINED is the physical counterpart that puts the data where the community lives, not where the funder sits. It is the place the other three campaigns plug into for storyteller recruitment, claim validation, and face-to-face outreach.

## Who (audience)

- Primary: the communities at each stop
- Secondary: the 10 anchor orgs in the Minderoo pitch (CONTAINED as a relationship tool, not just a data surface)
- Tertiary: visiting journalists, magistrates, academics, funders

## When (cadence)

Ongoing. Each stop: 2–6 weeks depending on site. Ben/Nick attend opening + closing; community hosts each stop.

## Success metric

- 8 stops completed by end of 2026
- Each stop produces: ≥3 consented storyteller captures, ≥1 claim file, ≥1 photograph essay, ≥1 local funder or magistrate visit logged
- At least 2 stops feed a campaign artefact in Aesthetics of Asymmetry (episode or secondary post)
