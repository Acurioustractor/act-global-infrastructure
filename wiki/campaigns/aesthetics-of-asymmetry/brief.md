# Aesthetics of Asymmetry — campaign brief

## What

A 6-month public art campaign that turns ACT's three platforms (CivicGraph + JusticeHub + Empathy Ledger) into a living data organism across six episodes + six printed artefacts. The art is the marketing. Word of mouth drives funding, partnerships, and pressure.

## Why

ACT has built the infrastructure to see information asymmetry in Australian civic data. The platforms alone won't change it. The campaign makes the asymmetry visible, forces public reckoning, and makes the stack impossible to ignore.

## Who (audience)

- Primary: ministers, ministers' staffers, magistrates, funders, journalists
- Secondary: community leaders, storytellers, academics, sector peaks
- Tertiary: aligned artists, adjacent campaigns

## When (cadence)

One artefact per month, pegged to the week's news. Each artefact = A2 poster + 16-page document + LinkedIn post. Secondary posts every two weeks.

## Success metric (single)

**Whether the Queensland government names the 22 organisations publicly before 30 June 2026.** That is the live trap Artefact 001 set. Everything else is colour.

Secondary: whether the ACT campaign model is picked up by one adjacent campaign before September 2026.
