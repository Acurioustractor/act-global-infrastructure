## Seeding discipline

- **Only add when there's a reason.** Someone commented publicly, emailed, attended, is about to be reached. No speculative contacts.
- **Capacity line:** what public role are they acting in. Not "nice person" — "CEO, Telstra Foundation".
- **Most recent interaction.** One line. Date.
- **Campaign(s):** which active campaign folder(s) reference them.
- **No private information.** Everything in this file could survive a FOI request.
