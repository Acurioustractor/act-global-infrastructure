# People Index

> `wiki/people/` is a curated layer for load-bearing people in the ACT ecosystem. It is not meant to mirror every storyteller, staff member, contact, or EL profile.

## System Position

The people layer follows the same split as the rest of the ACT system:

- the wiki holds durable people pages for figures who explain the ecosystem
- [[empathy-ledger|Empathy Ledger]] holds the broader live storyteller and portrait layer
- the websites compose a small number of public-facing people where that actually helps a page read better

This folder should stay closer to a cast list for the ecosystem than to a directory of everyone ACT has ever touched.

## Inclusion Rule

Create or maintain a `wiki/people/` page when a person is at least one of these:

1. a founder or core ecosystem operator
2. a load-bearing leader in a flagship or major project
3. a cultural authority or recurring community anchor whose role explains the project
4. a person whose story or practice materially explains ACT's method, art line, or public argument

Do **not** create a durable wiki person page just because someone:

- appears in one story
- has an EL profile
- is a staff member in a large organisation
- is mentioned once in a raw capture

When a person matters but is not yet load-bearing, keep them in:

- `wiki/raw/`
- `wiki/sources/`
- [[empathy-ledger|Empathy Ledger]]

Only promote them into `wiki/people/` when the wiki really needs them to explain the system.

## Rule of Thumb

If removing the person page would make a major project, cluster, or ACT method page harder to understand, the page probably belongs here.

If removing it would only make the wiki slightly more sparse, it probably belongs in EL or `wiki/sources/`, not `wiki/people/`.
