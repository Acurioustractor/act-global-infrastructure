# For Government | CivicGraph Australia

## Positioning

For Procurement Officers, Commissioners & Program Managers

> **Find the Right Supplier. Fund the Right Place.**
> 
> 672,000 contracts. 99,000 entities. 2,900 postcodes analysed. CivicGraph replaces spreadsheets and manual research with decision infrastructure — from supplier discovery to place-based allocation to outcome evidence.

## Stats

- 2,469 Government Grants
- 9 States Covered
- 108 Currently Open
- $215M Open Funding

## Products for Government

**Procurement Intelligence** — Discover suppliers across 99K entities. Check compliance from 20+ data sources. Generate bid-ready intelligence packs. Meet Indigenous procurement targets with verified data.

**Allocation Intelligence** — Place-based funding analysis across 2,900 postcodes. Gap scoring by SEIFA disadvantage and remoteness. See where money flows and where capability doesn't match need.

**Governed Proof (Coming)** — Rights-governed outcome evidence for renewal defence and policy justification.

## Workflow

1. Discover Suppliers — Search by entity type, geography, Indigenous status, contract history, and compliance signals.
2. Score Compliance — Cross-reference ACNC, ATO, ASIC, ORIC data. Generate risk and capability profiles.
3. Analyse Places — See funding by postcode, remoteness, SEIFA. Identify gaps between need and provision.
4. Defend Decisions — Export intelligence packs, place reports, and outcome evidence for audit and renewal.
