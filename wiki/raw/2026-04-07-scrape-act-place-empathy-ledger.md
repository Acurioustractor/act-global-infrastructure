# Empathy Ledger | A Curious Tractor Project Page

## Tagline

> In the space where individual narratives intersect with systemic understanding, a transformative potential emerges — not your story... not my story... but a third reality we can only discover together.

## Framing Contrast: Traditional vs. Transformative

**Traditional Impact Methodology (Extractive)**
- Researcher as Central Authority
- Hierarchical structure: information flows in one direction
- Subjects as Data Points
- Value extraction: benefits accumulate primarily at the institutional level

**Transformative Exchange Methodology (Empathy Ledger)**
- Storyteller (S) as Sovereign Center
- Network ecosystem: information flows through dynamic, living relationships
- Storyteller Sovereignty: people maintain control over their narratives
- Value Reciprocity: benefits flow to all participants in the ecosystem

## How It Works (4 Steps)

1. **SHARE YOUR STORY** — Tell your experience your way - voice, video, text, or photos. Set privacy controls - public, community only, or private.
2. **MAINTAIN CONTROL** — Edit, update, or remove your story anytime. Change who can see it as your comfort grows.
3. **CREATE VALUE** — Stories contribute to funding applications. Community patterns emerge from collective wisdom.
4. **BENEFIT FAIRLY** — Direct compensation when your story drives funding. Recognition as a knowledge holder and expert.

## Principles (Set by Communities, Not Tech Companies)

- **COMMUNITY OWNERSHIP** — Stories belong to storytellers and their communities. No extraction. No exploitation. Full sovereignty.
- **PRIVACY BY DESIGN** — You control every aspect of your story's visibility. Cultural protocols are embedded in the technology.
- **PURPOSE-DRIVEN** — Stories drive positive change, not charity marketing. Evidence for better policies, not poverty porn.
- **FAIR VALUE SHARING** — When stories create value, communities benefit. Transparent tracking of impact and returns.

> *These aren't our rules. They're community-set principles we've built technology to honour.*
