## Desired Impact Statement

> Our desired impact is a just, equitable, and regenerative world where every person and community can thrive.

## Fields (Domains We Work In)

- **Global Justice Innovation** — We harness the power of innovation, storytelling, nature, and art to catalyse transformative change in global justice.
- **Storytelling for impact** — Harnessing the power of narrative to inspire empathy, shift value afforded to those that have rather than those who are ready to be heard.
- **Nature for nurture** — Leveraging the wisdom and resilience of nature.
- **Art for conceptualisation** — Employing creative expression as a tool for envisioning alternative futures, sparking dialogue, and igniting imagination.

## Partnership Framing

**Community Partnerships:** At the heart of our work are the communities we serve. We recognise that the most effective and enduring solutions emerge when they are rooted in the lived experiences, knowledge, and aspirations of the people most directly impacted by the challenges we seek to address.

**Storytelling for impact:** We recognise that the challenges we face are complex and multifaceted, requiring a diverse range of skills, perspectives, and approaches to address.

**Nature for nurture:** To sustain and scale our impact over the long term, we need the support of values-aligned funders who believe in our mission.
