# About New | A Curious Tractor (Current About Page)

> A Curious Tractor (ACT) fosters co-creation between communities impacted by injustice and those who hold resources, skills, or influence. We provide a space for deep listening, radical collaboration, and iterative prototyping, ensuring that solutions are rigorously tested and refined based on the lived experiences of those they are meant to serve, resulting in shared ownership and genuine impact.

## Our Values (Publicly Stated)

- **Radical Humility** — We listen, resource, and step back, prioritising community-identified needs.
- **Decentralised Power** — We build systems that communities own, adapt, and govern.
- **Creativity as Disruption** — We use art and storytelling to challenge apathy and centre community voices.
- **Uncomfortable Truth-Telling** — We openly critique systems of oppression, including our own role.

## Our Approach (4 Steps)

**Community-Led (Listen)**
We start by listening deeply to the needs and aspirations of the communities we partner with. Every project begins with an explicit invitation, ensuring we're addressing genuine, self-identified challenges.

**Designed for Ownership (Co-Create)**
We believe in co-creation, working *with* communities, not *for* them, to design solutions. Our goal is for communities to ultimately own, adapt, and govern the tools and systems we build together.

**Iterative & Transparent (Prototype)**
We embrace a process of continuous learning, prototyping solutions publicly and sharing our progress openly. This includes acknowledging failures, adapting our approach, and celebrating successes alongside our partners.

**Focus on Long-Term Impact**
We're not interested in quick fixes; we're committed to fostering systemic change. We measure our success by the lasting, positive impact on communities and the dismantling of unjust systems.
