# About | A Curious Tractor — The Farm Ecosystem

## The Farm Ecosystem (Full Glossary)

The public-facing explanation of ACT's internal metaphor system:

- **The Soil** — Knowledge network. "We store our readings, retros, and projects all in this space, and we try to connect all the dots in unlikely ways. We all end up in soil one day."
- **The Seeds** — Active projects that need time, water, resources, and attention.
- **The Seed House** — Where we nurture and grow seeds that we are working on but are not in the field yet. Run and led by the farmers.
- **The Forest** — "Not all the wonders are lost — we need to get lost and reconnect with nature often."
- **The Campfire** — "This is the place we come around to share warmth and connect with people through conversation and ceremony."
- **ACT News** — Community engagement and ecosystem communication.
- **The Sun, The Seasons** — Values and what brings strength and joy.
- **The Seed Bank** — Projects we have seeded or are dreaming of seeding, but it's not the right season or conditions.
- **The Fields** — Domains and themes we are currently passionate about.
- **The Lab** — Where we experiment; projects come to life through events or with external people.
- **The Warehouse** — Where we store our parts and bits of projects.
- **The Tools** — "Our precious things that help us do our work. They are handed down from our grandparents, saved for, and refined."
- **The Bugs** — "This is what eats away at us but makes us stronger."
- **The Farmers** — Benjamin and Nic, who own and run A Curious Tractor. Focus on how to stay safe.
- **The Road Stall** — The dashboard on our website of our produce.
- **The Tractor** — Engine (how we run ACT), PTO/Power Take-Off (how we share energy), Our Brand, The Water Tank (resources distributed to fields and seeds).

## Origin Story (FAQ)

**What led to the birth of A Curious Tractor?**

> Born out of a shared desire to make a difference in the world through innovation, connection, and real human experiences. We realised that traditional approaches to change-making often lacked authenticity and genuine impact. We want to create a place where bold ideas can flourish, diverse perspectives could collide, and meaningful connections could be forged.

**How does ACT define innovation?**

> We define innovation as the art of uncovering new possibilities, questioning the status quo, and finding creative solutions to complex problems. It's about daring to think outside the box, embracing diverse perspectives, and fostering an environment where ideas can flourish. For us, innovation isn't limited to technology; it's a way of thinking that can transform any aspect of life.

**What's the "Raucousness" element?**

> The term "raucousness" reflects our willingness to challenge the status quo and venture into unconventional realms. It's about encouraging a bit of controlled chaos, where innovative thoughts and unconventional approaches are celebrated. This philosophy allows us to explore unexplored avenues, experiment with new possibilities, and approach challenges with a fresh perspective.

**What's the deal with tractors?**

> Tractors, oh tractors — they're more than just machines to us. They're the heartbeat of our connection to the land, the vessel for our dreams, and the source of transcendent thinking. Nestled in the heart of the farm in Witta, these mighty workhorses become more than just tools; they're the companions to Nic's boundless imagination.
> 
> You see, Nic's tractor rides aren't just about plowing fields — they're journeys into the uncharted territories of ideas. With the wind on his face and the earth beneath his wheels, he finds a symphony of inspiration that only the rolling hills can provide.
