# 2024 in Review | A Curious Tractor — Growing Curious

> Our Journey Through the 2024 Seasons

## Framing

> This year we've sought to power meaningful change while remaining grounded in relationships and respect. Each cycle has brought its own harvest of learning and been strengthened by the generosity of those who've shared their stories, dreams, and knowledge. Through this weaving of innovation and tradition, art and action, technology and human connection, we've seen how transformative change takes root when we create space for diverse voices to lead the way.

## Key Timeline Events (Extracted)

**January** — Confit and BG Fit in Mount Isa. G Code: Grounded, Goals, and Gratitude. Deep cultural knowledge and connection to Country. Brodie Germaine quote: "Being able to show Confit the beautiful country we have out here is deadly."

**January 28** — ACT Lab commences. Old shipping container transformation begins at the farm.

**February 4** — PRF fellowship application (later became Justice Co-lab at justiceco-lab.com).

**February 12** — Conversation Camp #1 on the farm. June's Patch and community wellbeing discussions.

**February 14** — DAD.LAB #1 at Black Cockatoo Valley. 14 Dads, inaugural session.

**February 28** — Last session at Acmena Detention Centre with Deadly Science (Kickin It STEM kits). 6 young people over 3 months, kits to travel into all Queensland youth detention centres.

**March 1** — SMART Stories campaign begins. Connection with SMART Recovery CEO April Long.

**March 5** — Con-nected v.01 final session (ANU students, Snow Foundation, Drug and Alcohol Sentencing Court Canberra).

**April 6** — BK visits Diagrama, Spain. "The most fundamental shift in his thinking about youth detention."

**April 23** — The Confessional and The Treacher completed.

**May 6** — Westpac Fellows at The Farm.

**May 15** — QLD Gives grant: $30,000 for June's Patch. Photo competition finalist (Confit/BG Fit collab).

**June 20** — Reintegration Puzzle talk + Gold Phone launch at Justice Reform Initiative conference. 80 people with lived experience on scholarships. Brodie Germaine's first keynote.

**July 2** — BK visits Kalgoorlie (first time). Meets Philip (Orange Sky driver) and Walter (Goods story).

**July 16** — AMP Spark intensive, Beechworth Prison.

**July 18** — ReKindle on ABC (holistic reintegration program).

**September 7** — BK touring Uluru to Darwin with AIME (Jack Manning Bancroft).

**September 24** — Visit Minjerribah Moorgumpin (Elders-In-Council) Aboriginal Corporation, Stradbroke Island.

**October 2** — Goods. Kalgoorlie trip. First meeting with Gloria and Walter. "Fundamental turning point in the roll out of the Greate Bed."

**October 20** — June Canavan Foundation Board meeting at the farm.

**November 3** — Goods. first trip to Bwgcolman (Great Palm Island). "Moving at the speed of ceremony." MingaMinga Rangers connection.

**November 20** — Mount Isa CAMPFIRE kick off. BK travels to Mount Isa. Elders, Traditional Owners, and young people.

**November 28** — AMP Spark Fellowship final pitch in Sydney. Brodie Germaine wins.

**November 29** — SMART Recovery 20th Anniversary + SMART Stories exhibition event.

**December 5** — Bimberi ReSOLEution and Confit. Young people in detention designing sneakers.

**December 10** — QLD Youth Justice Inquiry AI tool launch (interactive platform).

**December 16** — Back on Bwgcolman for year's final ACT trip.
