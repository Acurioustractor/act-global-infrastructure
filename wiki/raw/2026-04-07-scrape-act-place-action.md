2. **Ideate with Purpose: "Seed the Idea"** — Nurture creative ideas with intention.
3. **Forge Collaborative Paths: "Connect the Dots"** — Collaborate with diverse minds to connect ideas, experiences, and insights.
4. **Take Bold Action: "Fuel the Fire"** — Channel curiosity into fearless action.
5. **Elevate Impact: "Make Waves"** — Witness the power of curiosity-driven change.
