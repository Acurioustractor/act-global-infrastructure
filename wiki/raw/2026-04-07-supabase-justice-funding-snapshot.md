## Key Observations

- **QLD youth justice spending: $4.12B across 7,004 orgs** — yet portfolio analysis (ALMA) shows the highest-scoring interventions are run by small ACCOs typically funded for $50K-$5M each.
- **Philanthropic = $1.45B** — barely 5% of QLD community services alone. Philanthropy is a rounding error compared to government spend.
- **National reporting rows** are aggregate ROGS (Report on Government Services) — high-level, not granular.
- **ALMA linkage rate is high in QLD** (>90% of community services records linked to interventions), but zero in national reporting and most non-QLD states. This reflects the JusticeHub focus on QLD as the proof-of-concept jurisdiction.
