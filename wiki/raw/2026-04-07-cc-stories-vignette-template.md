## Guidelines

### Voice Owner and Authority

Be specific about:
- Who gave consent
- What their relationship to the story is
- How we know they hold authority

### Consent Scope Decisions

| Choose | When |
|--------|------|
| **INTERNAL ONLY** | Personal details, partner nuance, identity risk |
| **EXTERNAL-LITE** | System-level learnings, identity protected |
| **EXTERNAL** | Explicit consent complete, culturally reviewed |

### ALMA Signal Levels

| Level | Evidence | Authority | Harm Risk |
|-------|----------|-----------|-----------|
| **Low** | Anecdotal | External view | High risk |
| **Medium** | Documented | Practitioner | Moderate |
| **High** | Robust | Community-owned | Low risk |

### LCAA Stage

Tag where this story sits in the LCAA journey:
- **Listen:** Gathering understanding
- **Curiosity:** Testing and learning
- **Action:** Building and delivering
- **Art:** Making change visible
