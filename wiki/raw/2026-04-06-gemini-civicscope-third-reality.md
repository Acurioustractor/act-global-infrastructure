## Key Concepts Surfaced

### Civic World Model
Inspired by Block's "From Hierarchy to Intelligence" — CivicGraph replaces the "middle management" of the state with an interconnected intelligence layer. CivicGraph is the Capability and Intelligence Layer; JusticeHub, Empathy Ledger are Interfaces.

### The Third Reality
"Not your story... not my story... but a third reality we can only discover together." The synthesis when verified systemic data (CivicGraph) intersects with sovereign human narrative (Empathy Ledger). Formula: Systemic Data + Narrative Sovereignty = The Third Reality.

Proposed as registrable Trade Mark through IP Australia. Category Design strategy — position as the definitive alternative to broken impact reporting.

### Local AI Architecture for PICC
- Mac Studio / local edge compute — data never leaves community
- Gemma 4 E4B (4B params, runs offline, GGUF format)

### Karpathy LLM Knowledge Base Pattern
Raw data → LLM compiles wiki → agents do Q&A and enhance → viewable in Obsidian. The LLM maintains the wiki, humans curate sources. Applied to CivicGraph as "Civic Wiki" and to PICC as sovereign community archive.
