# ACT Ecosystem

> Six clusters, one connective tissue, one design principle — a map of how ACT's projects fit together and what holds them.

## The Shape of It

[[act-identity|A Curious Tractor]] is not a portfolio of separate programs. It is an ecosystem — each project feeds the others, shares infrastructure, and contributes to the same end: community sovereignty and the handover of genuine capability.

[[empathy-ledger|Empathy Ledger]] is the connective tissue. It flows stories into The Harvest, evidence into JusticeHub, community voices into PICC's annual report, and cultural knowledge into CivicGraph's picture of the system. Without Empathy Ledger, you have data. With it, you have the [[third-reality|Third Reality]].

## The Clusters

### 1. Narrative Sovereignty

**[[empathy-ledger|Empathy Ledger]]** — The platform that gives communities cryptographic ownership of their stories. Sovereign storytelling infrastructure designed for handover and operating under [[ocap-principles|OCAP principles]]. The EL Content Hub syndicates stories to The Harvest, JusticeHub, and partner platforms.

### 2. Evidence and Transparency

**[[justicehub|JusticeHub]]** — The evidence base for justice alternatives. 1,766 interventions catalogued. Supports communities forking, adapting, and sharing programs that work.

**[[civicgraph|CivicGraph]]** — 566K entities, 1.5M relationships: the civic intelligence layer. Who funds whom, who procures what, where government money flows and where it doesn't. Makes the system visible.

Together, JusticeHub + CivicGraph form the evidence layer: data that communities can use to advocate, funders can use to decide, and journalists can use to investigate.

### 3. Place

**[[the-harvest|The Harvest]]** — Regenerative community hub in Witta, QLD (former Green Harvest site). Seasonal kitchen, garden centre, workshops, venue hire. The physical expression of ACT's LCAA method: *Eat. Gather. Make. Grow.*

**[[act-farm|ACT Farm]]** — ACT's regenerative agriculture and creative practice site. R&D for land-based programs, therapeutic landscapes, and creative retreat.

**[[black-cockatoo-valley|Black Cockatoo Valley]]** — Long-form place-based project in the Sunshine Coast hinterland. Cultural and ecological restoration.

The Place cluster shares infrastructure (Witta Farm held in Nic's trust, leased to ACT) and methodology: regenerative land practice, community hospitality, and [[beautiful-obsolescence|Beautiful Obsolescence]] applied to physical infrastructure.

### 4. Community Control at Scale

**[[picc|PICC (Palm Island Community Company)]]** — The proof case. 176 staff, 85% Indigenous, 78% local. 100% community-controlled since 2021. Whole-of-life services: health, justice, family, enterprise. PICC is what community sovereignty looks like at scale, and it is the primary deployment site for ACT's [[../technical/local-ai-architecture|Local AI Architecture]].

### 5. Youth and Justice

**[[../communities/mount-isa|Mount Isa cluster]]** — BG Fit, CAMPFIRE, BAIL. Brodie Germaine's justice reinvestment network on Kalkadoon Country.

**[[goods-on-country|Goods on Country]]** — Place-based design and procurement. Circular manufacturing with communities.

**[[deadlylabs|DeadlyLabs]]** — STEM in youth detention.

**[[contained|CONTAINED]]** — Experiential design for youth justice advocacy.

### 6. Art and Culture

**[[uncle-allan-palm-island-art|Uncle Allan Palm Island Art]]** — Indigenous art and cultural sovereignty on Palm Island.

**[[the-confessional|The Confessional]]** — Safe space, vulnerability, community healing. Installation art.

**[[gold-phone|The Gold.Phone]]** — Nic's hacked vintage payphone connecting strangers globally. Thousands of authentic conversations as living archive.

**[[../projects/act-studio|ACT Regenerative Studio]]** — The coordination hub at act.place. Not just a website — the operational backbone connecting all clusters.

## How the Infrastructure Flows

```
Empathy Ledger (stories)
        ↓
  ┌─────┴─────┐
  ↓           ↓
JusticeHub  The Harvest
(evidence)  (community hub)
  ↓           ↓
CivicGraph  ACT Farm / BCV
(system)    (place)
  ↓
PICC
(proof case)
```

The ACT Studio coordinates across all of them. Every project can syndicate content via Empathy Ledger. Every project uses shared Supabase infrastructure (project `tednluwflfhxyucgwigh`).

## The Design Principle

[[beautiful-obsolescence|Beautiful Obsolescence]] applies to the ecosystem as a whole: the goal is not to grow ACT's footprint, but to transfer genuine ownership and capability until communities no longer need ACT. PICC is the furthest along this arc — 100% community-controlled since 2021. The Harvest is designed for eventual handover. Empathy Ledger is open source, designed to be forked.
