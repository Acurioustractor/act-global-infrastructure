# The Energy Orbit — how ACT builds, measures, and protects relational energy

> The orbit is everyone who brings ACT *doing, energy, and life*. [[relationship-first-crm|We sort people by energy, not by what they pay]]. This doc is the practice: how we see the orbit, how we've grown it, how we keep it warm, and how we stop it leaking — so energy turns into projects that fund themselves, not a treadmill that burns the founders out.

## The orbit, plainly

Picture ACT at the centre and the people around it in rings. The pull inward is not money — it is **reciprocal energy**: people we *do things with*, who bring life, who we'd call to build something. The inner ring is the **Getting Shit Done Alliance** ([[relationship-first-crm|`circle:gsd-alliance`]]) — hand-picked, never computed. As you move outward the relationships thin into the transactional: a cold connection request, a one-off ask, a newsletter open. **The transactional edge is fine and necessary. The work is keeping the centre relational, and making the centre also the place revenue comes from.**

Two lanes, never one funnel:
- **Supporter lane** — the rings above. Climbs by energy; this is what the orbit measures.
- **Community lane** — Oonchiumpa, storytellers, elders. A **parallel constellation**, co-owners held by consent and the [[ecosystem-value-exchange|OCAP-holds / CARE-owes]] frame. **Never ringed, never energy-scored, never in a drip.** Measuring community by "energy to ACT" is the exact extraction the whole model refuses.

## How we read it — the vibe pass

The "vibe pass" is a **metadata-only** sweep of the warm channels the email spine can't see — WhatsApp, iMessage, LinkedIn, Signal via Beeper. It reads *who / how often / how recently / how two-way*, **never message content**, and **only the supporter lane**. For each person:

- **Recency** — days since last exchange.
- **Frequency** — message volume (depth of contact).
- **Reciprocity** — the share you send vs receive. Balanced (~0.3–0.7) is a real two-way relationship; all-inbound is someone reaching toward you; all-outbound is you chasing.

These combine into a single orbit score (recency 40% · frequency 35% · two-way balance 25%) and a **pattern**:

| Pattern | Means | Energy reading |
|---|---|---|
| **generative** | reciprocal + active | energy *source* — the core |
| **inbound-lean** | they reach, you rarely reply | energy *asked of you* — gold or drain; needs a human read |
| **outbound-lean** | you chase, little back | energy *spent* — a one-sided pursuit |
| **cooling** | was deep, gone quiet | latent energy — worth re-warming |
| **light** | one or two touches | the transactional edge |

**Three honesty rules, or the number lies:**
1. **The score measures *channel intimacy*, not worth.** WhatsApp/iMessage score high because that's where real talk happens; LinkedIn scores low because it's a doorway, not a lounge. A low LinkedIn score (George Newhouse, Scott McDougall, Phoebe Frederick) means *"not our channel with them yet,"* not *"low value."*
2. **It's a prompt for judgement, not a verdict.** Energy can't be computed — the Alliance stays [[relationship-first-crm|hand-curated]]. The pass surfaces candidates; a human decides.
3. **It's one lens of three.** Beeper = informal channels. The [[#relates-to|Gmail spine]] = the two-way email network (264 people, funder-heavy). GHL = the system of record. The orbit is real only when all three are reconciled onto one person.

## How we've built energy — the evidence

The orbit didn't come from a CRM. It came from **shared doing**. The pattern repeats across every project:

- **Contained (JusticeHub)** — you don't pitch the youth-justice story, you put people *inside* it. The visceral shared experience makes champions, and the champions become the network. The current Adelaide / Victoria Square push is a working group of people who felt it together.
- **Community Capital / Caravan Collective retreat** — a few days together turned into durable bonds ("beautiful memories of a very special few days"). The *experience* was the relationship engine.
- **Goods on Country · Harvest · Witta** — building on the land with people. The garden crews, the "Witta Harvest Adventure" (a kid who "loved it, keen to come again") — place-based doing that bonds families, not transactions.
- **Empathy Ledger** — storytelling as the renewal mechanism: people show who they are, *really well, and they own it*. Energy flows back to the person; [[relationship-first-crm|case studies prove the venture]] for ACT. One engine, two outputs.
- **Snow Foundation** — proof that a funder relationship can be genuinely warm and aligned (easy banter, reconciled tranches). When alignment is real, **energy and revenue arrive together**.
- **Soleful crew / dreaming sessions** — peer energy with people like Ben Croft: Bali dreaming, the Greens event, "keep this dream rocking." No transaction, pure orbit.
- **Oonchiumpa** — the guardrail case. A deep partnership that must **never** be pulled toward an ACT-centred funnel; it is held as a sovereign constellation. Energy here is *mutual obligation*, not pull-toward-centre.

## How we keep building it — connected experiences

The future isn't more outreach. It's more *experiences that bond*, and a system that recognises them. Four mechanisms:

1. **Pods as first-class, not just individuals.** The bonding unit is the small group doing a thing — the Contained crew, the retreat, the garden crew, Soleful. The CRM should hold the *experience that bonded people*, not only the person (a `pod:` alongside `circle:gsd-alliance`).
2. **Storytelling as renewal.** People sharing their story in service of what they're passionate about refills energy *and* makes them feel **seen, not segmented**. Pointed inward at the network, not only outward at funders.
3. **Membership as mutual commitment, decoupled from money.** Support mechanisms and groups to help each other get what we all want — belonging you opt into, not a tier you buy.
4. **Peer support, not hub-and-spoke.** People supporting *each other*, so Ben and Nic aren't the only energy source. ACT convenes; it doesn't gatekeep from the centre.

## Where energy comes from — and where it's taken

The reason to map this at all: **protect the founders' energy and the revenue at the same time.** They are not in tension — misaligning them is the failure mode.

**Energy sources (invest here):**
- Generative-pattern people on the warm channels — the reciprocal core.
- Pods mid-build (Contained now) — shared momentum is renewable.
- Aligned funders (Snow) where the cheque *and* the relationship are real.
- Storytelling moments — they pay energy back.

**Energy drains (name them, don't absorb them):**
- **The philanthropy treadmill.** The email spine skews funder-heavy; the doers are buried. Chasing misaligned funders for survival drains energy and lets them set the agenda. Discipline: keep the misaligned *light and transactional* without guilt; don't let a grant become the identity ([[four-lanes|Grants is one lane of four]]).
- **Money stress as an energy tax.** Runway and overdue invoices are themselves a drain (the daily money brief is the canary). The answer is not "chase more grants" — it's making the **generative core the revenue core** through commercial pipelines that pay the founders ([[relationship-first-crm|Goods, Harvest — money as plumbing]]).
- **Inbound-lean asymmetry.** One-sided demand (the Peter Norden pattern). Triage it: convert the *aligned* ones to two-way (Phoebe, Terry, Anurag are latent gold), let the rest stay light. Not every inbound deserves your energy.
- **Community-as-management.** Spending energy "running" community through funnels is both extractive and exhausting. Releasing it into genuine partnership returns energy (fix the [[ecosystem-value-exchange|Kristy/Oonchiumpa drip violation]]).

**The maintenance loop:** run the vibe pass periodically → concentrate time on the generative-and-revenue-aligned → convert latent inbound that fits → keep community sovereign → let the cold edge stay cold. Energy in, sustainable projects out, more energy back.

## The operating loop this enables

The orbit is the read; the system is the loop. Sources → one orbit → a home + a tag for every real person → comms and visualisation that fit the ring → match people to what each project actually needs → bring the shown energy in.
