# LCAA Method

> Listen. Curiosity. Action. Art.

## Definition

LCAA is A Curious Tractor's foundational methodology — the operating system for how ACT approaches every project, community, and challenge.

## The Four Phases

### Listen
Start by deeply listening to community. Not surveying, not consulting — listening. Understanding what already exists, what's working, what hurts, what matters. Reject the impulse to arrive with solutions.

### Curiosity
Follow the threads that emerge from listening with genuine curiosity. Ask better questions. Explore unexpected connections. Resist premature closure. This is where innovation lives — in the space between what is and what could be.

### Action
Move from understanding to doing. Build, prototype, test, iterate. Action is collaborative — done with community, not to community. Bias toward making things real, not just planning.

### Art
The final transformation — where action becomes something beautiful, meaningful, and lasting. Art is how impact is communicated, how culture is preserved, how stories change the world. Art is not decoration — it's the highest expression of the work.

## How LCAA Maps to the Ecosystem

| Phase | Primary Platforms | Expression |
|-------|------------------|------------|
| Listen | Empathy Ledger, field research | Sovereign storytelling, community voice capture |
| Curiosity | CivicGraph, JusticeHub, ALMA | Data exploration, evidence review, pattern discovery |
| Action | Goods on Country, PICC, BG Fit | Place-based design, service delivery, enterprise |
| Art | Empathy Ledger, Uncle Allan, CONTAINED | Photography, installation, exhibition, narrative |

## LCAA as Taxonomy

Every project, every piece of art, every community relationship can be tagged with where it sits in LCAA:
- Some projects span all four (e.g., PICC work: listen to Elders → curiosity about what works → action through services → art through Photo Kiosk)
- Some live primarily in one phase (e.g., CivicGraph is deep Curiosity infrastructure)
- The wiki uses LCAA tags to surface connections between projects
